BrightSign is the global leader of media players (or simply, *players*) for digital signage. We also offer tools for player management, content authoring, and content management.

Our products are highly differentiated. Given the unique nature of our products, combined with their extensive functionality and flexibility, it is beneficial for users to have a general, high-level understanding of our product’s capabilities and how they work. *Most new users to BrightSign will find it very useful to go through this short (8-12 minute) overview.*
