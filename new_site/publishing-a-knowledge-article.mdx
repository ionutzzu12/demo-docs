When a knowledge article goes through the approval process and is approved, the system updates the status of the knowledge article to reviewed. A \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}} then has the option to review the knowledge article again or promote it to the "Published" status.

Only a \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}} can promote an article to the "Published" status.

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} as a \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}.
2. Open the **Knowledge** workspace or select the **Knowledge** tab in a parent business object.
3. Select the knowledge article.
4. From the **Status** field drop-down list, select **Published** or click **Publish Knowledge Article** from the toolbar.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The system updates the **Published Date** field with the current date.
</Paragraph>
