\{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}} can create additional statuses to meet the business needs of your organization.

<hint type="info">
  - **Notifications** show that the email is sent to the assigned reviewer based on customer configuration.
  - The listed **roles&#x20;**&#x68;ave access to the knowledge workspace.
</hint>

| State     | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       | Notifications                                                                            | Roles                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Draft     | Articles are set to draft when initially created. Users in the Self-Service Portal cannot search the articles in draft state, but other users can.                                                                                                | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}} when article is saved               | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}}<br />\{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}<br />\{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Manager}} |
| In Review | Articles are set to in review when the owner is ready to have it reviewed. Users in the Self-Service Portal cannot search the articles in the in review state, but other users can.<br /><br />This state starts the knowledge approval workflow. | No notifications                                                                         | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}}<br />\{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}<br />\{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Manager}} |
| Reviewed  | Article has been approved.                                                                                                                                                                                                                        | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}                                     | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}                                                                                                                                                  |
| Published | Articles are set to published when the reviewed article is published, which means that it is available in the Self-Service Portal.<br /><br />Only Knowledge Managers can update articles in the published state.                                 | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}<br />\{\{FRS\_VARS.Article\_Owner}} | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}                                                                                                                                                  |
| Expired   | The article is obsolete but can be searched for by internal users.                                                                                                                                                                                | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}                                     | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}                                                                                                                                                  |
| Archived  | The article is no longer part of the Knowledge Base. Only Knowledge Managers can search for it.                                                                                                                                                   | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}                                     | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}                                                                                                                                                  |
| Rejected  | The article has been rejected after review.                                                                                                                                                                                                       | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Article\_Owner}}                                                           | \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Knowledge\_Manager}}                                                                                                                                                  |
