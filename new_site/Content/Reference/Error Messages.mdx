## Self Service Role Has No Rights to Delete ServiceReqParam\#

*System Exception: Object Delete*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/vKg42uZJpy3KFmFAchG0e_errorobjectdelete.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../Resources/Images/help-images/error_objectDelete.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/error_objectDelete.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

This message occurs when a user in the Self-Service Portal has the ServiceReqParam and ServiceReqParamLink business object permissions not set to delete and attempts to delete a saved service request.

*Self Service Permissions*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/0yo9GvMLP8b-PmV149zCQ_servicereqparampermissions.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../Resources/Images/help-images/ServiceReqParam_permissions.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/ServiceReqParam_permissions.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

To fix this, do the following:

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Configure > Users and Permissions > Roles and Permissions** to open the **Roles and Permissions** workspace.
2. Click **SelfService**.
3. Click the **Object Permissions** tab.
4. For the ServiceReqParam and ServiceReqParamLink business objects, check **Delete** (the last checkbox).
5. Click **Save**.
