You can track the progress of an email created by a business object. For example, say that you have an incident that generated an email through a workflow.

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Open the **Incident** workspace.
3. Open the incident to troubleshoot.
4. Click the **Journal** tab to view the emails sent and received.

*Journal Tab Shows Emails Created*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/CC7d1qWmVrN8Ci59MIMOO_email-generated-by-workflow.png" alt="The Journal tab shows emails sent and received." position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/email generated by workflow.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/email generated by workflow.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  In this example, the last email in the list was generated by a workflow, but you can also track emails created within the incident record (refer to [Using Email](../../ServiceDesk/Using_Email.mdx)).
</Paragraph>

5. Select the email from the list, then click **Go to**. The application opens the **Email** workspace.
6. At the bottom of the window, click the **Send Info** tab. The application displays the email information.

*Send Info Tab*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/ELMjVrmqLv8Ddkdm3NJ2P_email-send-info.png" alt="Image of the Send Info tab." position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/email send info.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/email send info.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The value of the **Sent** parameter is **false**, which means that the email did not go through. The application lists the number of attempts and shows any errors.
</Paragraph>
