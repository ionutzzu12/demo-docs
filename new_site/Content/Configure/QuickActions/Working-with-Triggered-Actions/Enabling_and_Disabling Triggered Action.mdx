createdAt: Tue Jun 30 2026 17:16:57 GMT+0300 (Eastern European Summer Time)

You can enable or disable Triggered actions created for business objects. By default, the application enables the Triggered actions when they are created.

To enable or disable a triggered action:

1. In the Graphical Action Designer (GAD), select th&#x65;**&#x20;Saved Actions** tab.
2. Select **Triggered Actions&#x20;**&#x66;rom the category, and click the arrow icon to display the list of triggered actions.
3. Select the Triggered actions from the list, and click :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/xpsziUn708A1NfO1np_wt_enable-disable19x14.png" alt caption size="10" width="19" height="14"} to enable or disable the triggered action.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/PRmGnKgz79vleSZuatpRh_saved-actions.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../../Resources/Images/help-images/Saved Actions.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/Saved Actions.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  A confirmation message appears when you have enabled or disabled the triggered action.
</Paragraph>

<hint type="info">
  You can also enable or disable a trigger action . See [Trigger Action Rules](https://help.ivanti.com/ht/help/en_US/ISM/2023/admin/Content/Configure/BusinessRules/Business_Rules.htm?Highlight=business%20rules#4Enabling) .
</hint>
