### To Edit a Webhook:

1. In the **Settings&#x20;**&#x6D;enu, click **Webhooks Manager** in the **Build&#x20;**&#x73;ection.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The **Webhooks Manager** opens.
</Paragraph>

2. Click anywhere in the row for your chosen webhook.
3. On the **Update Webhook** page, edit the information in the fields.

<hint type="info">
  **Note:** The endpoint extension is case-sensitive.
</hint>

4. Click **Save**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  A confirmation message appears saying **Webhook successfully updated**.
</Paragraph>

### To Delete a Webhook:

1. Select one or more webhooks to be deleted.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/UZfocIG682IV6AIrvlRr4_deletingwebhooks.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="images/deleting_webhooks.png" githubPath="Content/Configure/Webhooks/images/deleting_webhooks.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

2. Select **Delete**.
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
