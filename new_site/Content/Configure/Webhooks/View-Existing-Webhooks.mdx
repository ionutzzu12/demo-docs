1. In the **Settings&#x20;**&#x6D;enu, click **Webhooks Manager** in the **Build&#x20;**&#x73;ection.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/WPdIIbKzoT03CIltVNDH6_viewwebhooks.png" alt="View existing webhooks in a list" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="images/view_webhooks.png" githubPath="Content/Configure/Webhooks/images/view_webhooks.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The **Webhooks Manager&#x20;**&#x73;creen opens. The associated **Action&#x20;**&#x6E;ame is shown in the second column.
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/s7AsQbYb_OJNrJ_32FFOY_viewwebhooks2.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="images/view_webhooks2.png" githubPath="Content/Configure/Webhooks/images/view_webhooks2.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

2. To enable or disable a webhook, select the toggle for your chosen webhook.
3. To show more webhooks on the screen, select **10 25 50 100&#x20;**&#x61;t the bottom of the screen.
4. To sort a column, hover over a column and select the up/down arrow for the column.
