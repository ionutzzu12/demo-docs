This example uses an incident but the procedure applies to other business objects.

\{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} has a selection of over 170 default icons. You can also create and import your own icons.

In this example, the numbers (1, 2, 3, 4, and 5) in the **Priority** column (see figure below) are replaced by the following new icons: :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/oUuzMYJqC9qR6PCTjkGu4_priorityiconsset23x2.png" alt caption size="10" width="23" height="2"}

*Default List with Priority Numbers*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/tHMT5WB6KgJLOuVUTM6Uf_prioritycolumnnumbers.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/priority_column_numbers.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/priority_column_numbers.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

Before you begin this procedure, you must add any new icons to the Image Manager. See :Link[Using the Image Renderer]{label="Using the Image Renderer" overridedLabel="Using the Image Renderer" spaceId docId="XlFI06jFSJ4Fi7rfNCxi1" version="v2" docAnchorId="#AboutImage" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="Lists.htm#AboutImage" href="Lists.mdx"}.

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Build > Business Objects** to open the **Business Objects** workspace.
2. Open the **Incident** business object.
3. Click the **Lists** tab. The application displays a set of lists created for the **Incident** business object.
4. Open the list to edit.
5. In the list, choose a column to edit.
6. On the right, in the **Image** field, click the **function** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/nkqElOFXdxvDGVcRO7D1F_function.png" alt caption size="10" width="16" height="16"}.
7. In the Expression Editor, enter an expression that displays each icon in the place of a number.

*Example of an Expression*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/L8W2vYIeqfqs7x4IJ914q_expressionpriorityicons.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/expression_priority_icons.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/expression_priority_icons.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

8. Click **Save**.
9. In the List Editor, click **Save**.
10. In the \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} application, refresh the browser. The new icons now appear in place of numbers in the column list.
