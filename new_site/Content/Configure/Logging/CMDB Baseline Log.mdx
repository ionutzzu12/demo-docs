The **CMDB Baseline** log is included by default and lists the baseline IDs for specific business objects.

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Monitor > Application Logs > CMDB Baseline Log** to open the **CMDB Baseline Log** workspace. The list of IDs appears.

*Sample CMDB Baseline Log*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/bPradCMlCdOxhVyHC07yV_cmdb-baseline-log.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/cmdb baseline log.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/cmdb baseline log.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

2. To delete a log from the CMDB baseline log, do the following:

<Paragraph indent="1">
  a. Select the item to remove.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  b. Click the **delete** icon <Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/USPl9Egu4Y2Blx7HKC3mA_deleteredx.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/buttons/delete_red_x.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/buttons/delete_red_x.png" width="14" height="13" showCaption="false"></Image> from the toolbar.
