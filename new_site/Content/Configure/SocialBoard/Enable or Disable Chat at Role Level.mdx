---
title: Enabling/Disabling Chat for a Role
docTags: 
createdAt: Tue Jun 30 2026 17:16:51 GMT+0300 (Eastern European Summer Time)
---

1. From the Configuration console, click **Build > Page Layouts**.
2. Select the role from the **Roles** column for which to disable the **Chat** feature.
3. In the **Page Layouts** column, locate **Social Board**, scroll to the right, and click **Advanced Options**.
4. Select or clear the **Disable Chat** check box to enable/disable **Chat** feature.
5. Click **Save**.
6. Return to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} console and refresh the browser.
