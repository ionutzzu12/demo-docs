title: Adding the Share Button to a Header Form
docTags: 
createdAt: Tue Jun 30 2026 17:16:51 GMT+0300 (Eastern European Summer Time)

You can add a special **Share** button to the header forms for business objects, such as incidents or changes, to invoke the corresponding quick action for posting a message relative to the given record.

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Build > Business Objects** to open the **Business Objects** workspace.
2. Open a business object.
3. From the **Forms** tab, choose the form to which to add the **Share** button.
4. Create space for the **Share** button, such as adding columns to the form.
5. From the left pane, expand the **Other** folder, then navigate to Social Share Button.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  *Social Share Button*
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/pGHFyyIjG6J44lr6MRx_P_social-share-button.png" alt="Image of the Social Share button." position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/social share button.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/social share button.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

6. Drag **Social Share Button** onto a space in the header form and drop it. The **Share** button usually appears to the right of the label expression.
7. The button size by default is defined as 12em. This might be too wide, so you can change the width to 5em, from the **Control Properties** pane.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  *Control Properties Pane*
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/p5wAl1JOHoY73gs6fsAcY_social-share-properties.png" alt="Image of the Control Properties pane." position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/social share properties.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/social share properties.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The button width adjusts.
</Paragraph>

8. Associate the **Share** button with the appropriate quick action, (for posting the message to the Social Board) by selecting **Post Social message associated with Incident**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  *Share Button Quick Action*
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/0nogmVhQe02TnAtMi6YBo_share-button-quick-aciton.png" alt="Associating the Share button with a quick action." position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/share button quick aciton.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/share button quick aciton.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

9. Click **Save**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The **Share** button now appears in the application ready for you to post messages to the Social Board.
</Paragraph>
