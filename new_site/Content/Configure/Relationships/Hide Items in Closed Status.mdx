You can hide related items in the *closed* status from certain users.

1. From the Configuration Console, add a relationship from a business object to a configuration item business object. See :Link[Adding a Relationship to a Business Object]{label="Adding a Relationship to a Business Object" overridedLabel="Adding a Relationship to a Business Object" spaceId docId="bbN-X_29QOEX_8YXjBBrX" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Adding" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="Adding_a_Relationship_to.htm#Adding" href="Adding_a_Relationship_to.mdx"}.
2. Enter information into the fields.
3. In the **Condition** field, enter **$(\[OtherObject]Status != "Closed")**.
4. Click **Save**.
5. Click **Save** again.
