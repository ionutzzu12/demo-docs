## Creating a Supporting Organization

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Configure > Organizational Profile > Supporting Organizations** to open the **Supporting Organizations** workspace.
2. Click **New Supporting Organization**. A blank form appears.
3. Enter a unique name for the new supporting organization.
4. Click **Save**. The new organization appears on the list.

## Deleting a Supporting Organization

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Configure > Organizational Profile > Supporting Organizations** to open the **Supporting Organizations** workspace.
2. Select the organization to delete.
3. Click **Delete**. The organization is no longer on the list.
