You can configure the look and feel of the Self Service Mobile portal using the **Self Service** tab in the **Style Editor**.

The following are the steps to customize the Self Service Mobile portal:

1. From the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Configuration\_console}} , click **Build** > **User Interface Styles** > **Style Editor**. The application displays the **Style Editor** page.
2. Click the **Self Service** tab.
3. Use the options in this tab to customize the Self Service Mobile portal to suit your taste.

<Paragraph indent="1">
    <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="1">
      **Edit Self Service Widget** - takes you to the Self Service Mobile portal in edit-mode where you can design the Self Service Mobile dashboards for different Self Service Mobile extended roles.
    </Paragraph>  <Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="1">
      **Edit Enterprise Self Service Page** - takes you to the Enterprise Self Service page in an edit-mode where you can design the page.
    </Paragraph>
</Paragraph>

<hint type="info" indent="2">
  To restore the **Self Service Mobile** portal to its default configuration, click the **Remove Customization** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/8MTnlIsqNFdggXIj_Vr-B_removecustomizationicon23x23.png" alt caption size="10" width="23" height="23"} at the top of the **Style Editor** page, next to the **Role** list.
</hint>

## Adding a New Logo to the Self Service Mobile Portal

In the **Self Service Mobile** portal, logos are scaled to fit a 27-pixel vertical space in the header. Because scaling reduces image quality, the preferred logo dimensions are 27 pixels high and up to 200 pixels wide. The maximum logo dimensions are 256 pixels high by 256 pixels wide.

To add a new logo to the **Self Service Mobile** user interface, do the following:

1. Access the Style Editor. See :Link[Accessing the Style Editor]{label="Accessing the Style Editor" overridedLabel="Accessing the Style Editor" spaceId docId="qoeST1R7XXj46m2YXpkpu" version="v2" docAnchorId="#AccessingTheStyleEditor" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="Style Editor.htm#AccessingTheStyleEditor" href="Style Editor.mdx"}.
2. Click the **Self Service** tab.
3. From the **Header** section, click **Upload file**.
4. Click the **Select File** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/46JidEisQOn8omfny09Cu_logofoldericon.png" alt caption size="10" width="16" height="16"}.
5. Browse for the logo on your to add and double-click the file name.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The application displays the new logo in place of the default logo and on the header in the right pane.
</Paragraph>

</hint>

6. Click **Save**.

### Restoring the Default Logo for the Self Service Mobile User Interface

To restore the default logo for the **Self Service Mobile** user interface, do the following:

1. Access the Style Editor. See :Link[Accessing the Style Editor]{label="Accessing the Style Editor" overridedLabel="Accessing the Style Editor" spaceId docId="qoeST1R7XXj46m2YXpkpu" version="v2" docAnchorId="#AccessingTheStyleEditor" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="Style Editor.htm#AccessingTheStyleEditor" href="Style Editor.mdx"}.
2. Click the **Self Service** tab.
3. In the **Header**, click the current logo.
4. Click the **Delete Uploaded File** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/e-zlSKp1AvX6F9KZjn0t5_logodeleteicon.png" alt caption size="10" width="16" height="16"}.
5. The application deletes the current logo and displays the default \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} logo in its place.

<hint type="info" indent="1">
  The application marks the unsaved value with a red triangle :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/EHiyYfMVQWob8kZOS2-vm_styleeditorredtriangle.png" alt caption size="10" width="7" height="14"} at the left and the **Revert** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/uzqDpiJCmf14BiE72Ypet_stylereverticon.png" alt caption size="10" width="17" height="16"} at the right.
</hint>

6. Click **Save**.
