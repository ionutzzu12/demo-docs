You can change the way \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} looks, including its colors, fonts, and logos. All styles comply with standard \{\{CSS}} rules and can be defined with or without expressions (see [About Using Expressions in Styles](<../../Reference/Expressions/Using Expressions in Styles.mdx>)).

There are different ways to use styles in \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}:

- **Highlighter Styles**: (formerly known as the Grid Highlighter). You can create your own styles or use the default styles to be applied to lists, forms (see [Using Forms](../Forms/Forms.mdx)), and controls. See [Using Highlighter Styles](<Using Highlighter Styles.mdx>).
- **Style Editor**: You can define the look and colors of the overall application, including logo, headers, footers, panels, and workspaces. See .
