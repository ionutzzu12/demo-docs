You can organize your block templates into categories by creating category folders and moving the block templates into the relevant folders. This feature is helpful if you create a large number of templates and want to categorically organize them.

## Creating a Category Folder for Block Templates

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} Self Service Mobile portal.
2. Change your role to \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}.
3. From the **Home** page, click the gear icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/3M1-R4UE-zFYmbQrJA_fX_settings-icon.png" alt caption size="10" width="20" height="17"}.
4. Click **Parts** to view the list of block templates.
5. Click the **Manage Categories** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/dxNnhdtRlqx-q2Hg-eV9N_managecategoriesssm23x22.png" alt caption size="10" width="23" height="22"} > **Add**.
6. Enter a title for the category folder. The title can have a maximum of 50 characters. Each folder must have a unique name.
7. Click **Save**. The application displays the new category folder.
8. Click **Close**. The application displays the new category folder at the top of the **Parts** list.

## Placing a Block Template into a Category Folder

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} Self Service Mobile portal.
2. Change your role to \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}.
3. From the **Home** page, click the gear icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/3M1-R4UE-zFYmbQrJA_fX_settings-icon.png" alt caption size="10" width="20" height="17"}.
4. Click **Parts** to view the list of block templates.
5. In the **Parts** list, select the block template you wish to add to a category and click the **Edit** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/mOv-znjoS_I8ZoCkFHcif_editcategoryssm.png" alt caption size="10" width="16" height="16"}.
6. In the **Category** field, click the **search** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/Uj2DaMngxVW0bh6puJIH7_searchssm.png" alt caption size="10" width="16" height="16"}.
7. Choose a category folder from the list and then click **Select**.
8. Click **Save**. The block template is added to the selected category.
9. Click the arrow icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/veoP9h9fbiHSzSWTDpWnC_arrowheadfolderssm.png" alt caption size="10" width="9" height="15"} beside the category to expand and view the block templates added to the category.

## Removing a Block Template from a Category Folder

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} Self Service Mobile portal.
2. Change your role to \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}.
3. Click the arrow icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/veoP9h9fbiHSzSWTDpWnC_arrowheadfolderssm.png" alt caption size="10" width="9" height="15"} or double-click the category folder to open it.
4. Select the template and click the **Edit** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/mOv-znjoS_I8ZoCkFHcif_editcategoryssm.png" alt caption size="10" width="16" height="16"}.
5. In the **Category** field, click the **Clear** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/H7jFuSHBeIrAPLcEfsUuB_clearcategoryssm23x23.png" alt caption size="10" width="23" height="23"}.
6. Click **Save**. The block template is removed from the category and is displayed in the **Parts** list.

## Changing the Name of a Category Folder for a Block Template

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} Self Service Mobile portal.
2. Change your role to \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}.
3. From the **Home** page, click the gear icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/3M1-R4UE-zFYmbQrJA_fX_settings-icon.png" alt caption size="10" width="20" height="17"}.
4. Click **Parts** to view the list of block templates.
5. Click the **Manage Categories** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/dxNnhdtRlqx-q2Hg-eV9N_managecategoriesssm23x22.png" alt caption size="10" width="23" height="22"}.
6. Select the category folder and click the **Edit** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/mOv-znjoS_I8ZoCkFHcif_editcategoryssm.png" alt caption size="10" width="16" height="16"}.
7. Enter a new title for the category folder.  The title can have a maximum of 50 characters. Each folder must have a unique name.
8. Click **Save** and then click **Close**.

## Deleting a Category Folder for a Block Template

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} Self Service Mobile portal.
2. Change your role to \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}.
3. From the **Home** page, click the gear icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/3M1-R4UE-zFYmbQrJA_fX_settings-icon.png" alt caption size="10" width="20" height="17"}.
4. Click **Parts** to view the list of block templates.
5. Click the **Manage Categories** icon:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/dxNnhdtRlqx-q2Hg-eV9N_managecategoriesssm23x22.png" alt caption size="10" width="23" height="22"}.
6. Choose a category folder and click the **Delete** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/ja2ZlyZswWZz7q5qv7D5Z_deletecategoryssm.png" alt caption size="10" width="15" height="14"}.
7. Click **Yes** to confirm and then click **Close**.
