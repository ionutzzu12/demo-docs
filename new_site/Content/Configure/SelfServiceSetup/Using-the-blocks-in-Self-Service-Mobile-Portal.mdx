You can use the blocks in the Self Service Mobile portal and also customize them by adjusting their size to suit your need.

## Adding a Block

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} Self Service Mobile portal.
2. Change your role to \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}.
3. From the **Home** page, click the gear icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/3M1-R4UE-zFYmbQrJA_fX_settings-icon.png" alt caption size="10" width="20" height="17"}.
4. Click **Parts** to view the list of block templates.
5. Drag a block template from the list to the home page.
6. Position the template relative to an existing block using the placement tool.

## Resizing a Block

You can resize any block in the **Home** page of the Self Service Mobile portal. When you change the size of a block, the application adjusts the sizes of other blocks, in an attempt to keep all blocks visible in the **Home** page.

### Adjusting the Height of a Block

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} Self Service Mobile portal.
2. Change your role to \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}.
3. From the **Home** page, click the gear icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/3M1-R4UE-zFYmbQrJA_fX_settings-icon.png" alt caption size="10" width="20" height="17"}.
4. Click **Preview**.
5. Position your cursor at the bottom of the block to resize. The cursor changes to a double-arrow :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/ZWh2nScaU2AyiPvS3TsS5_bannerresize1ssm.png" alt caption size="10" width="15" height="16"}.
6. Click the mouse. The cursor changes to a pointer.
7. Drag the bottom of the banner up or down.

### Adjusting the Width of a Block

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} Self Service Mobile portal.
2. Change your role to \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}.
3. From the **Home** page, click the gear icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/3M1-R4UE-zFYmbQrJA_fX_settings-icon.png" alt caption size="10" width="20" height="17"}.
4. Click **Preview**.
5. Position your cursor at the right edge of the block to resize. The cursor changes to a double-arrow :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/K3-fh91PbJoLVNwjzmVhZ_bannerresize3ssm.png" alt caption size="10" width="15" height="16"}.
6. Click the mouse. The cursor changes to a pointer.
7. Drag the right edge of the banner left or right.

## Editing Blocks

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} Self Service Mobile portal.
2. Change your role to \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}.
3. From the **Home** page, click the gear icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/3M1-R4UE-zFYmbQrJA_fX_settings-icon.png" alt caption size="10" width="20" height="17"}.
4. Click **Preview**.
5. Click the **Settings** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/xJW1_6fIiGiCIIUIXbuF0_settingsssm.png" alt caption size="10" width="17" height="16"} of the block to edit.
6. Make necessary changes.
7. Click **Save**.
