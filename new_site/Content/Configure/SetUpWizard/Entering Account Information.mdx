This is where you set the default account information to use throughout the \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} application, including your default time zone and currency.

<hint type="info">
  This is the first step in the application setup process. For more information about the entire process, including how to access this page, refer to [Setting Up the Application](<Setting Up The Application.mdx>).
</hint>

The following are the steps to enter your account information:

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Configure > Organizational Profile > Application Setup**.
2. Click **Your Account**. The application displays the **Application Setup - Organization Information** page.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  *Application Setup - Organization Information Workspace*
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/OFpdUP2SUDQyNOF3acZhj_enteraccountinformation.png" alt="The Application Setup - Organization Information workspace." position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Configure/EnterAccountInformation.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Configure/EnterAccountInformation.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

3. Enter information into the fields.

| Field                                                                      | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| What is the name of your company?                                          | The name of the tenant organization.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| What is the preferred time zone for outbound email for your organization?  | The time zone for outgoing email. This time zone is the default used in email actions related to the application, such as quick actions. Refer to [Working with Dates, Times, and Time Zones](<../../ServiceDesk/Dates/Working with Dates Times and Time Zones.mdx>). |
| What is the preferred date/time format to display dates in outbound email? | The date/time format for outgoing email. This is also the default format used in reports and dashboards.                                                                                                                                                              |
| What is the preferred currency symbol to display in the application?       | The currency symbol to use. You can set a single currency for the tenant. The selected currency appears in the **Request Offering** and **Service Catalog** workspaces. Check **Display currency symbol** to display the currency symbol.                             |
| What are the standard hours of operation?                                  | The hours of operation for the tenant organization. Enter the days, times, and time zone. Also refer to [Setting Up Hours of Operation](../HoursOfOp/Hours_of_Operation.mdx).                                                                                         |

4. Click **Next** to save your settings and go to the next page. Refer to [Adding Customer Information](<Adding Customer Information.mdx>).

## Modifying Account Information

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Configure > Organizational Profile > Account Information**.
2. Make changes as needed.
