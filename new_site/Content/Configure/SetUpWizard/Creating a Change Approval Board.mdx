Each step of the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Change\_Enablement}} setup process is carried out on through the **Application Setup -&#x20;**\{\{FRS\_VARS.Change\_Enablement}} page. To get to the setup page:

1. Log in as an \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}} and open the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Configuration\_console}}.
2. From the **Settings** pane, select **Configure** > **Organizational Profile** > **Application Setup**.
3. In the **Application Setup** workspace, select **Change Management** to display the **Application Setup - Change Enablement** page.

## Creating a Change Approval Board

Before this step in the setup process, identify users to participate in your Change Approval Boards (CABs). \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} provides three default CABs; each one can have different and overlapping members.

- Change Approval Board: CAB for non-emergency changes.
- Emergency Change Approval Board: CAB for emergency changes.
- Management Committee: Management Committee for major changes.

## **Managing CAB Groups and Members**

Use the Application Setup - Review Board page to manage your CABs. The page contains a list of Groups and Members. Groups comprise CABs, management groups, and more. Members are the individual users in your organization that you will assign to the CAB groups.

### Managing Groups

Look for the three default CABs in the list of groups; you can add and edit groups like CABs and Review Boards, but \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} does not allow you to delete groups. The application displays Groups in the Groups pane of the **Application Setup - Review Board** page.

**Editing Group Details**

1. Highlight one of the groups.
2. Click **Rename**.
3. Make changes to the Group name and details or to the change type this Group reviews.
4. Click **Save**.

**Adding a New Group**

1. Click **Add Group**.
2. Enter a Group name and description.
3. Select the change type that this Group reviews.
4. Click **Save**.

### Managing Members

The application displays members of **Groups** in the **Members** pane of the **Application Setup - Reivew Board** page. From this page, you can add **Members** to **Groups** or remove them.

**Adding a new&#x20;****Member****&#x20;to a&#x20;****Group**

1. Highlight one of the review boards in the Groups pane.
2. Click **Add Member**.
3. Choose a search category, enter the search criteria, and click **Go**.
4. Highlight the name of the person you want to add to the review board Group.
5. Click **Link**.

**Removing a Member from a Group**

1. Highlight one of the review boards.
2. Click **Unlink** next to the name of the Member you want to remove from the Group.
