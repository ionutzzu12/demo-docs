1. From the Configuration console, click **Configure > Organization Profile > Business Hours**.
2. Open a schedule to modify.
3. Click the **edit**:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/WTLKbMa5PI7qeazzbj0FD_edit-old.png" alt caption size="10" width="16" height="16"} icon. The application displays the **Edit Hours of Operation** window.
4. Modify the information in the fields as need be. For more information on the fields, see [Creating an Hours of Operation Schedule](Creating-Hours-of-Operation-Schedule.mdx).
5. Click **Save**.
