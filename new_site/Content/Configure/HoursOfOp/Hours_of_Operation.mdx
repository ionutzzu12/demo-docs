The **Business Hours** feature allows you to define the operational calendar for your organization, including daily hours and schedules.

The **Business Hours** definition help calculate the response time (such as escalations). For example, you can determine the incident response time for a technician or execute business rule that escalates a call that has been open for a number of hours.

Further, you can add holidays or days when the office is closed, to the calendar. You can have multiple lists, each with a unique name.

You can view a Business Hours definition from the Configuration console > **Configure > Organization Profile > Business Hours** > select a schedule to view its definition.
