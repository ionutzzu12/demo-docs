---
title: Deleting a Form
docTags: 
createdAt: Tue Jun 30 2026 17:16:56 GMT+0300 (Eastern European Summer Time)
---

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Build > Business Objects** to open the **Business Objects** workspace.
2. Open a business object.
3. Click the **Forms** tab. The application displays a list of forms for that business object.
4. Hover the mouse over a form name and click the **Delete Form** icon.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  *Delete a Form*
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/3AulP6FQGDi8kwDJcdstX_form-delete.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/form delete.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/form delete.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The application displays the **Delete confirmation** dialog box.
</Paragraph>

5. Click **Yes**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The application checks whether the form is used in another business object. If so, a validation message informs you that the form cannot be deleted and where the form is used. If this form is not used in another object, the application deletes the form.
</Paragraph>
