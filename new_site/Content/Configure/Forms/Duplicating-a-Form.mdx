title: Duplicating a Form
docTags: 
createdAt: Tue Jun 30 2026 17:16:56 GMT+0300 (Eastern European Summer Time)
---

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Build > Business Objects**.
2. Open a business object.
3. Click the **Forms** tab. The application displays a list of forms for the business object.
4. Move your mouse pointer over a form name and click the **Copy Form** icon.

  *Copy Form Button*
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The application displays the **Enter the Name** window.
</Paragraph>

5. Enter a unique name for the new form.
6. Click **Save**. The application displays the new form in the form list.
