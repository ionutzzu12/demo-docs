**Role**: Administrator

**Minimum Version**\{\{Primary.Company}} \{\{Primary.Product}} 2024.3.

Use the **Page Designer** to edit page design and configure page elements for \{\{Primary.Company}} \{\{Primary.Product}} \{\{FRS\_VARS.Self\_Service\_Portal-Self-Service}}.

## To open the Page Designer:

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} \{\{FRS\_VARS.Self\_Service\_Portal-Self-Service}} portal, and then change the role to Administrator.
2. Click **Edit Page** at the top right of the window. The **Page Designer** opens.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/aaL9_aRpl17zrOIzD-gs9_page-designer-new.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/SelfServiceMgmt-images/Page Designer New.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/SelfServiceMgmt-images/Page Designer New.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

3. Use the **Page selector** to select the page you want to edit.
4. Use the **View selector** to view the page in **Desktop**, **Mobile**, or **Tablet** design mode.
5. Click the following tabs to customize the workspaces.
   - Page Settings
   - Global Settings
