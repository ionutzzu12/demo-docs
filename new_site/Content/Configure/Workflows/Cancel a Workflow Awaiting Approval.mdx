---
title: Example: Canceling a Workflow that is Awaiting Approval
docTags: 
createdAt: Tue Jun 30 2026 17:16:58 GMT+0300 (Eastern European Summer Time)
---

You can cancel a service request, change, incident, or other business object while it is in the pending approval state.

1. Log in to the Service Desk Console.
2. Open a workspace.
3. Open the record that is waiting for approval (pending approval).
4. From the **Status** field, choose **Cancelled**.
5. Click **Save**. The **Cancellation Reason** window appears.

*Enter Cancellation Reason*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/_6dHUHNX7MkII6wTtBjth_cancel-reason.png" alt="Enter Cancellation Reason" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/cancel reason.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/cancel reason.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

6. Enter a reason for the cancellation.
7. Click **OK**. The application cancels the request and terminates the approval workflow.
