---
title: Deleting a Field
docTags: 
createdAt: Tue Jun 30 2026 17:16:55 GMT+0300 (Eastern European Summer Time)
---

1. From the Configuration console, select **Build > Business Objects**.
2. Select any business object to open the business object page.
3. Select the **Fields** tab to display a list of fields.
4. Select the **delete** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/USPl9Egu4Y2Blx7HKC3mA_deleteredx.png" alt caption size="10" width="14" height="13"} at the end of the row associated with the field you want to delete.
5. Select **Yes** to confirm the deletion.
