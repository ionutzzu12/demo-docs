Everything begins with business objects. After you create your own business object, there are several things you can do with it.

- [Working with Business Objects](Business_Objects.mdx): A business object is the basic building block.
- [Using Fields](Using-Fields.mdx): A field contains the values that you use within a business object.
- [Using Lists](../../ServiceDesk/Working-with-Lists/About-Lists.mdx): A list shows your data in a tabular format.
- [Using Forms](../Forms/Forms.mdx): A form shows your data in a format that users can enter data into.
- [Using Layouts](../Layouts/Layouts.mdx): A layout, also known as a workspace, is where you place the list or form.
- [About Workflows](../Workflows/Workflows.mdx): A workflow creates a set of actions that occur when an action occurs, such as when a record is saved or a status is changed.
