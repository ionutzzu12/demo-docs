\{\{FRS\_VARS.Company}} \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} allows you to resize and enlarge text fields. For example, while reviewing an incident containing multiline fields, you might not be able to see all of the content due to the small area. You can enlarge the text area by dragging the corner of the text field to better use the screen area.

1. From the **Incident** workspace, open an **Incident**.
2. Use the re-sizable handle on the bottom right corner of the **Description** field to re-size it.
   Drag-out to expand and drag-in to reduce the field size.

<hint type="info">
  The application retains the new size as you change through different incidents when you are in the same workspace.
</hint>
