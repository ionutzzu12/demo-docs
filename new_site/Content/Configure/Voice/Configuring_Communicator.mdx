To make and receive calls using the **Voice** toolbar, you must start **Communicator** before you log into \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.

Communicator settings are configured by default. However, you must enter or verify your user information and phone extension.

When the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Company-short}} Voice server is running and your settings are correctly configured, the system displays a checkmark in the status bar at the bottom of the **Communicator** window.

## Configuring Communicator Settings

1. From the Windows Start menu, select **All Programs > Ivanti > Communications Management > Communicator**. The system starts Communicator.
2. From the **Information** tab, click **Settings**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  *Communicator Settings*
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/F1yVSVOEwgd65N_troWzL_communicatorsettings.jpg" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/communicator_settings.jpg" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/communicator_settings.jpg" showCaption="false"></Image>

3. Click the **SoftPhone** tab, enter the extension of the user in the **Number** field, and enter the login name in the **Display Name** field.
4. Click **Apply**.
5. Click the **SIP Registration** tab.
6. Ensure that the IP address and port numbers are accurate.
7. Enter information into the **User Name** and **Password** fields.
8. Click **OK**.
