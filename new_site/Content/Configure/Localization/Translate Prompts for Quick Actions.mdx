## Translating Quick Actions

You can translate quick actions through the Quick Actions Center.

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Build > Automation Tools > Quick Actions** to open the **Actions Manager** workspace. The application displays the **Actions Manager** workspace, listing the business objects that have quick actions.

*Quick Actions Manager Workspace*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/gsrVx11OWoQl_jtAJ5C6r_qamanager.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/qa_manager.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/qa_manager.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

2. Next to the business object to translate, click **Translation Tool**.
3. Select the language to translate to. The application opens the **Translation Tool**.
4. Enter your translated text as described in [Using the Ivanti Translation Tool](<Using the Translation Tool.mdx>).

## Example: Translating the Prompt for an Incident Quick Action into French

1. From the Configuration Console, click **Build > Automation Tools > Quick Actions** to open the **Actions Manager** workspace. The application displays the **Actions Manager** workspace, listing the business objects that have quick actions.
2. Next to **Incident**, click **Translation Tool**.
3. From the drop-down list, select French. The application opens the Ivanti **Translation Tool**.

*Translating the Incident Business Object to French*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/lB9NQti9TPkvHMCfGkyVh_qa-manager-translate.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/qa manager translate.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/qa manager translate.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

4. Scroll down through the list, until in the **English** column, you see **$(Prompt("Assign a Team",...) ...)**.
5. Click the corresponding text in French to select it.
6. Enter the translated value. For example, enter **$(Prompt("Affecter une equipe", "Team of StandardUserTeam"))**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The first parameter (in this example, "Affecter une equipe") contains the translated value. The second parameter (in this example, "Team of StandardUserTeam") contains the validation list name. Keep the validation list name in English to ensure that the back-end processes perform correctly.
</Paragraph>

*Sample French Translation of a Prompt*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/MAH7e-akArc9LFO4vSJ17_prompt-translate.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/help-images/prompt translate.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/help-images/prompt translate.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

7. Click **Save**.
