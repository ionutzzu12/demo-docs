Existing \{\{Primary.Product}} customers have to configure the CI Business Object Layout and add the Patch Info child tab, since it is not available OOTB by default.

1. In the Configuration Console, select **Build** > **Business Objects**.
2. Search and open the **CI** Business Object.
3. Click **Layout** from the top menu and open the Layout that is used for the CI Business Object.
4. Select **Computer** from the **View in this Layout** section.

*Setting up the Layout in the Configuration Console*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/5UxK7FwaehQxYakbDhpZK_view-in-this-layout-computer.png" alt="Selecting Computer from the View in this Layout section." position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Configure/Patch-Integration/View-In-this-Layout-Computer.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Configure/Patch-Integration/View-In-this-Layout-Computer.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

5. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click **Add Child Panel**.
6. Enter the following information:

<Paragraph indent="1">
  a. In the **Display Name** column, enter the name of the tab as **Patch Info**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  b. In the **Object** column, click on **Not Set**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="2">
  The **Form View Child Panel Relationship Edition** window is displayed.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" indent="3">
  In the **Form View Child Panel Relationship Edition** window, select **ivnt\_PatchInformation (via ivnt\_PatchInformationAssocCI** for **Direct Relationship**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="2">
  *Configuring Options in the Form View Child Panel Relationship Editor*
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/veQGL8W1boR4vb7GOW4hE_patch-info-tab-releationship.png" alt="Selecting options in the Form View Child Panel Relationship editor." position="flex-start" size="40" indent="3" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Configure/Patch-Integration/Patch-Info-Tab-Releationship.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Configure/Patch-Integration/Patch-Info-Tab-Releationship.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

<Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" listStart="2" listRestartPolite="2" indent="3">
  Click **Save**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  c. Scroll to the right and click **Edit**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="2">
  The **Toolbar Editor** window is displayed.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" indent="3">
  In the **Toolbar Editor**, remove the unwanted buttons and keep only the **Quick Action Custom\:Get\_latest\_Patch**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="2">
  *Configuring Options in the Toolbar Editor*
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/_cl0v5TMtOcmxQcaB5OMf_patch-info-setup-toolbar-editor.png" alt="Selecting options in the Toolbar Editor." position="flex-start" size="40" indent="3" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Configure/Patch-Integration/Patch-Info-Setup-Toolbar-Editor.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Configure/Patch-Integration/Patch-Info-Setup-Toolbar-Editor.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

<Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" listStart="2" listRestartPolite="2" indent="3">
  Click the **Edit** :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/pUcDCwFqa9aQFPQhBx47U_edit.png" alt caption size="10" width="15" height="16"} icon in the **Toolbar Editor**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="3">
  The **Toolbar Button Editor** window is displayed.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="decimal" listStart="3" listRestartPolite="3" indent="3">
  Enter the following information in the **Toolbar Button Editor** window:
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="4">
  **Display** - **Text Only**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="4">
  **Tooltip** - **Get Latest Patch Info**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="4">
  Click **Save**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  d. In the **List** column, select **PatchDetailsList**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  e. In the **Initially Visible&#x20;**&#x63;olumn, set as **True**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  f. Click **Save**.


</Paragraph>

*Configuring Options to Ensure Patch Updates will be Visible*

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/nAiOq5lN5920oUUaakROm_patch-info-child-tab-set-up.png" alt="Ensuring that the patch information will be visible in the ITSM user interface." position="flex-start" size="40" indent="2" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/Configure/Patch-Integration/Patch-Info-Child-Tab-Set-Up.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Configure/Patch-Integration/Patch-Info-Child-Tab-Set-Up.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The Layout set up is done and now the **Patch Info** child tab will be available in your CI Business Object.
</Paragraph>
