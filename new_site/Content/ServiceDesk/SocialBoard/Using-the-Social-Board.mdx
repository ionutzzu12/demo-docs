The Social Board is the workspace used to chat with one or more people at a time. The following are the features of the Social Board:

- You do not automatically see chats sent to you unless you open the **Social Board** workspace or you have the **Social Board** open.
- You can only have ten conversations in the Social Board at one time.
- After you have opened the **Social Board** workspace (which activates the Social Board), you can close the **Social Board** workspace and continue to send and receive chats.

## Accessing the Social Board

The administrator must grant you access to the **Social Board** workspace before you can use it. If you do not see the **Social Board** button, then your role does not have access to this feature.

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Click on the icon in the top bar to open the **Social Board** workspace.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  After you open the Social Board, you can send and receive messages and use the Chat Zone.
</Paragraph>

For more information on setup and configuring options in Social Board, refer to [Working with the Social Board](Working-with-Social_Board.mdx).

## Using the Social Board

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Open the **Social Board** workspace.
3. Click **Start new conversation** in the Social Board to begin a chat.
4. To add users, do the following:

<Paragraph indent="1">
  a. Click **Add Person** and enter the name of the person to chat with.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  b. Click **Add**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  c. Repeat to add more users to the conversation. There is no limit to the number of users that can be added.
</Paragraph>

5. Type a message in the message box and press the **ENTER&#x20;**&#x6B;ey to publish the message.
   - Public conversations have a white background.
   - Private conversations have a blue background.
6. To remove the conversation, click the X icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/b5B1ltbEFlXIOTuCxc0U7_removeconversation.png" alt caption size="10" width="18" height="14"}.

## Opening a Conversation in the Chat Zone

After you have published a conversation, you can open it in the Chat Zone so that you can monitor a specific conversation.

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Open the **Social Board** workspace.
3. Locate your conversation and click this icon:inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/hWNJJhjfcw5tJsbZTo8gc_upe-popoutconversationicon.png" alt caption size="10" width="32" height="20"}.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/LHbKVXZm92uGQRJckjL6E_upe-poppingoutconversation.png" alt="button for popping out a conversation from the social board into the chat zone" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/buttons/UPE-poppingoutconversation.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/buttons/UPE-poppingoutconversation.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The Chat Zone opens containing your conversation. You can open multiple conversations from the Social Board in the Chat Zone.
</Paragraph>

4. To remove the conversation from the Chat Zone, minimize it by clicking the up arrow and then click the X.

## Chatting with a Team, Group, or Role

You can have a conversation at the team level in the Social Board. You can only chat with teams, groups, or roles of which you are a member.

Before chatting with your team, group, or role members, you must start the conversation on the Social Board.

<hint type="info">
  You can only share text messages and not images or other files.
</hint>

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Open th&#x65;**&#x20;Social Board&#x20;**&#x77;orkspace.
3. Enter a message in the text box.
4. Click **Add group**.
5. Choose a team, group, or role from the drop-down list, then click **Add**.
6. Click **Share**. The message appears on the Social Board workspace and the conversation is submitted to the team.
