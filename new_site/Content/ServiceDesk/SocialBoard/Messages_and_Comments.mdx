You can broadcast messages on the Social Board to all \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} users, a group of users, or even to specific users which appear on their **Home** page as soon as the users log in to the application.

## Types of Social Board Messages

There are two types of messages in the Social Board:

- **Public**: Messages that can be read by all the users.
- **Limited**: Messages that can only be read by specific users, groups, teams, or roles.

## About the Social Board counter

The **Social Board** icon is an unread message counter which is a quick and easy way to post and view messages. Click the **Social Board** :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/DJ_-xWTydHODuTyVVwsXf_upe-newonsocialboardicon.png" alt="Icon showing how many social board messages there are waiting to be read" caption size="10" width="28" height="31"} icon to open the unread message counter to view the unread messages and post messages on Social Board.

The following are the features of **Social Board** counter:

- The number of unread messages is shown next to the **Social Board** icon. If the Social Board has not been activated, you do not see a number.
- The message counter increments each time a new message is posted but does not include your own messages.
- By default, the **Everybody's activity** area and your comments get updated instantly, while the **Teams Activity** area gets updated every five minutes. The maximum number of notifications shown is ten.
- You can see when a new message has been posted even if you are in another workspace. This eliminates the need to switch to the **Social Board** workspace to check for new messages.
- To clear the **Social Board** counter, either open the message window or click **Clear & Close**.

## Viewing Social Board Messages

You can view the Social Board messages either from the **Social Board** workspace or from the Chat Zone.

<hint type="info">
  Chat can only be accessed after the Social Board has been activated.
</hint>

## Filter and view Messages in Social Board

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Open the **Social Board** workspace.
3. Choose the filter criteria from the left **Activity Stream** pane. The messages are categorized as follows:
   - **Everybody's Activity** - Messages published to all users.
   - **Teams Activity** - Messages published to your team.
   - **Groups Activity** - Messages published to a group that you are a part of.
   - **My Activity** - Messages published only to you.

## Posting a Message to the Social Board

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Open the Social Board workspace.
3. Click within the **Share what's new** message text box. The **Publish to** section appears.
4. Enter your message in the text box.
5. You can choose whom to publish the message.
   - To broadcast a message to all users, use the default setting which is **Everybody**.
   - To send a message to specific groups, roles, or teams, click **Add group** > choose a group, role, or team from the drop-down list > click **Add**.

<Paragraph indent="2">
  Repeat to add more groups, roles, or teams.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
  To send a message to a specific person, click **Add person** > enter the name of the user in the text box > **Add**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="3">
  Repeat to add more users.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  *Publish To*
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/P0YptSLrVEE3tF-rKCYLm_postmessage.png" alt="Social board message post showing distribution choices" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/post_message.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/post_message.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

6. Click **Share**. The message is sent to the users/groups/teams based on your selection.

3. In the **Publish to:** section, click the **Delete** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/USPl9Egu4Y2Blx7HKC3mA_deleteredx.png" alt caption size="10" width="14" height="13"} next to the name of a user, group, team, or role.

## Posting a Comment to a Social Board Message

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Open th&#x65;**&#x20;Social Board** workspace.
3. Click in the message text box and enter your message.
4. Either click **Share** or press the **ENTER&#x20;**&#x6B;ey.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The comment is shared to the users receiving the original message.
</Paragraph>

## Deleting a Social Board Message or Comment

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Open the **Social Board&#x20;**&#x77;orkspace.
3. Click the **Delete** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/M2ztJ2a59rChGjMyA5wXX_deleteblack.png" alt caption size="10" width="15" height="16"} on the right hand side of the post or comment header.
4. Click **Yes** for the prompt message.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The message/comment is deleted.
</Paragraph>

<hint type="info">
  You can only delete the message you posted and other what other users posted.
</hint>
