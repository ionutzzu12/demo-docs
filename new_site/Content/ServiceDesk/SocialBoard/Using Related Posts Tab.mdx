Related posts appear under the **Related Posts** tab. If a knowledge article, change, incident, problem, or release record is created from the Social Board, the posting appears under the corresponding business object.

You cannot create, modify, or delete posts from this tab.

See [Creating a Record from Social Board](Creating_Record_from_Social_Board.mdx).
