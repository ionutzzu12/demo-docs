The \{\{FRS\_VARS.Payroll\_Manager}} is responsible for Timesheet Management, hence the related workspaces - **Timesheet Cost Center**, **Timesheet Details**, **Timesheet Logs**, and **Timesheet Project** are available only for the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Payroll\_Manager}}. These workspaces provide timesheet-related details for processing payroll.

## Timesheet Cost Center

In this workspace, the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Payroll\_Manager}} can create cost centers for the organization. Cost centers help measure the consumption or expenses incurred by different business units or departments which are recognized by a cost center.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Viewing, editing, and creating a cost center

  1. Open the **Timesheet Cost Center** workspace.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    A list of existing cost centers is displayed.
  </Paragraph>

  2. Select one to edit or view its details.
  3. Click **New** to create one.
  4. Enter the **Name**, **Code**, and **Owner** for the cost center.
  5. Select **Link** or **Unlink** button to tag or un-tag projects to the cost center.
  6. Click **Save**.
</ExpandableHeading>

## Timesheet Details

In this workspace, the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Payroll\_Manager}} can view timesheet details at project level.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## To view the timesheet details of a project

  1. Open the **Timesheet Details** workspace.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Timesheet records by project are displayed.
  </Paragraph>

  2. Open a record to view the details.
</ExpandableHeading>

## Timesheet Logs

In this workspace, the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Payroll\_Manager}} can view timesheet logs for employees.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## To view a timesheet log

  1. Open the **Timesheet Logs** workspace.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Timesheet records for employees are displayed.
  </Paragraph>

  2. Open a record to view the details.
</ExpandableHeading>

## Timesheet Project

In this workspace, the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Payroll\_Manager}} can create timesheet projects for the organization. Timesheet projects are useful to track and measure the expenses incurred for the project.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## To create and edit a timesheet project

  1. Open the **Timesheet Project** workspace.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    A list of existing projects is displayed.
  </Paragraph>

  2. Select one to view and/or edit.
  3. Click **New** to create one.
  4. Enter the **Name**, **Code**, **Start Date** and **End Date** of the project, and other information.
  5. Click **Save**.
</ExpandableHeading>
