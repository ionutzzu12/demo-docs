The Service Requests placed by \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} users and by the HR team that are related to Human Resource department are logged as HR Requests.

## Creating an HR Request

1. Open the **HR Request** workspace.
2. Click **New**.
3. Enter details in the fields:
   - **Customer** - the customer's name for whom the request is placed.
   - **Team** - the team assigned to the request.
   - **Owner** - the HR team member assigned to the request.
4. Set the **Urgency** based on the priority.

## Working with the HR Request

An HR Request may need several tasks to be completed and may undergo many approvals before it is fulfilled.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Assigning an Owner

  When HR Requests are created by non-HR users, the owner is not assigned, it is normally assigned by the \{\{FRS\_VARS.HR\_Manager}}. But when a request is raised internally (from the HR Request workspace by the HR team), the **Owner** field is mandatory to log the request.

  1. Open an HR Request.
  2. Assign an owner in the **Owner** field and click **Save**.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Create a task

  1. Open an HR Request.
  2. Click the **New Task** button.
  3. Enter the **Summary**, **Team** and other details in the **New Task** window.
  4. Click **Save**.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    To perform other actions such as re-assigning a task or canceling a task, go to the [Task](HR-Task.mdx) workspace.
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Viewing approvals for a request

  1. Open an HR Request.
  2. Click the **Approval** tab.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    The approval records for the request are automatically created based on the request template and the approval statuses are also updated automatically.
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Link and unlink HR Case to a request

  1. Open an HR Request.
  2. From the **HR Cases** tab, click the **Link** button.
  3. Select the HR Case to link and click **Ok**.
  4. To unlink an HR Case, select the case to unlink and click the **Unlink** button.
</ExpandableHeading>
