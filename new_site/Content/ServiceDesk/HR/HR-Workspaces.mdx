The HR-specific workspaces in \{\{Primary.Product}} cater to HR roles and functions, each workspace has a different function. The workspaces visible to you and accessible by you are configured by your organization's \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}} or \{\{FRS\_VARS.HR\_Administrator}} based on your user role and responsibilities.

To know more about each workspace, click on the respective link below:

- [HR Case](HR-Case.mdx) - Employee grievances are logged as HR Cases. A case owner is assigned to resolve it.
- [HR Requests](HR-Requests.mdx) - Employee \{\{FRS\_VARS.Service\_Requests}} that are specific to the HR department are logged as HR Requests. An owner is assigned to track all required approvals, tasks and finally fulfill the request.
- [Task (HR Task and HR External Task)](HR-Task.mdx) - All HR cases and requests will have tasks added to resolve and fulfill the case or request. These tasks could be for the HR team or non-HR teams.
- [HR Approvals](HR-Approvals.mdx) - Approvals required to fulfill HR-specific requests are tracked.
- [HR Announcement](HR-Announcement.mdx) - Announcements from the HR department can be created by the \{\{FRS\_VARS.HR\_Administrator}} or \{\{FRS\_VARS.HR\_Manager}}.
