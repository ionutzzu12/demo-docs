All the requests that need approval from you, if you are the approving manager, are displayed in this workspace.

To approve or deny a request:

1. Open the **Approvals** workspace.
2. Open an approval request.
3. Click **Approve** or **Deny**.
4. Enter the reason for approving or denying in the pop-up dialog box.
5. Click **Approve** to approve the request and **Deny** to deny the request.
