As a \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}}, you can use the DEX Score to prioritize Incidents. For information on what DEX scores are, refer to [About DEX Scores.](https://help.ivanti.com/ht/help/en_US/CLOUD/vNow/dex-scores.htm)

A DEX Score in red color code indicates more issues in the user's device requiring to be prioritized higher than a DEX Score in green color which has fewer issues.

The DEX Score is calculated on the devices associated to a user. Every time the device is synced from \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons}} to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}, the score is recalculated and displayed in the Employee workspace list view, Employee details view, \{\{FRS\_VARS.Incident}} list view, and the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Incident}} details view.

<hint type="info">
  This feature is available only if your \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}} has specifically configured and has imported devices from \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons}} to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
</hint>

The DEX Score is rated from 0-100 and has the following color codes:

- Red - has 5 or more warning indicators.
- Yellow - has 3-4 warning indicators.
- Green - has 0-2 warning indicators.

## Sample Scenario

Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} as a \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}}.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Incident workspace
</Paragraph>

1. Open the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Incident}} workspace.
2. In the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Incident}} list view, check th&#x65;**&#x20;DEX Score** column.
3. Sort the incidents by DEX Score for better insights.
4. Pick incidents that have the highest DEX Score to be addressed first since they have more issues than the others.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  You can also view the DEX Score in the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Incident}} details workspace.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Employee workspace
</Paragraph>

1. Open the Employee workspace.
2. In the Employee list view, check the **DEX Score** column.
3. Sort the employees by DEX Score from lowest to highest,

<Paragraph indent="1">
  This gives you a better insight into employees having the lowest scores.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  You can also view the DEX Score in the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Incident}} details workspace.
</Paragraph>
