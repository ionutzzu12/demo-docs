When a user calls the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} to report an incident, the application automatically sets the incident owner to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}} who logs the incident and sets the team to the team that the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}} belongs to. If the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}} can resolve the incident for the user, it is considered a first call resolution and the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}} and his team remain the owner and team to whom the incident is issued.

If the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}} needs to reassign the incident to another team, the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}} must manually change the team and owner of the incident.

When an end user submits an incident through the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Self\_Service\_Portal-Self-Service}} portal or through email, the application puts it into the logged state. The \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Manager}} uses saved searches, such as the saved searches called **All Incidents Waiting for Analyst** or **All incidents Breaching in the Next 24 Hours**, to find unassigned incidents and assigns them to owners.

After the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Manager}} assigns the incident, the application notifies the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}} that the incident has been assigned to him and notifies the end user that the incident is being worked on .

1. Log in to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} console.
2. Open the **Incident** workspace.
3. Open an incident.
4. From the toolbar, click **Assign**.

## Reassigning an Incident

<hint type="info">
  The escalation clock for the incident does not stop when an incident is reassigned.
</hint>

1. Log in to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} console.
2. Open the **Incident** workspace.
3. Open an incident.
4. From the **Team** drop-down list, select the team to reassign the incident to.
5. From the **Owner** drop-down list, select the person to reassign the incident to.
6. Click **Save**.
