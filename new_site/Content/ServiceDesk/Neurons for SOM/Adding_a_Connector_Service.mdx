Add Connector Service information for Jira or ADO so the Ticket Sharing and Synchronization (TSS) service can find it.

1. Sign in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} as an \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}. Open the Connector Service workspace.
2. If the required service (Jira or ADO) is not listed, select **New Connector Service**.
3. In the **Service Name** field, enter **ADO&#x20;**&#x6F;r **Jira**.
4. **Save**.
5. In the Credential Parameters tab, ensure the following parameters are selected:

<Paragraph indent="1">
  **Service URL**
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  **Username**
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  **Password**
</Paragraph>

6. In the **Supported Operations** tab, select the following options:

<Paragraph indent="1">
  **Push Comments added in HEAT to Service**
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  **Pull Comments added in Service to HEAT**
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  **Push Attachments added in HEAT to Service**
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  **Pull Attachments added in Service to HEAT**
</Paragraph>

7. **Save**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The newly created Connector Service is now available in TSS.
</Paragraph>
