Use the Audit Calendar to view/edit a calendar of proposed and actual start and end dates for Audits.

- Open the Audit Calendar workspace to view the Audit dates as **GRC Manager** or **GRC Analyst**.
- To enable editing records from the Audit Calendar, you must be logged in with the **GRC Manager** role.
- Double-click a calendar entry to open the record for editing.
