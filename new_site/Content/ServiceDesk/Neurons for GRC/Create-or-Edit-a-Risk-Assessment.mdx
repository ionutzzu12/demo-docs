Create a Risk Assessment to discover, correct, and prevent security problems. Complete analysis questions and calculate Risk scores (mitigated and unmitigated). Accept the Risk and mitigate with Controls or a Mitigation Plan, or transfer or avoid the Risk.

<hint type="info">
  To create or edit a Risk Assessment, you must log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} as a **GRC Manager**.
</hint>

## Create a Risk Assessment

**To create a Risk Assessment:**

1. Open the **Risk Assessment** workspace.
2. Click **New GRC Risk Assessment** to open a blank **Risk Assessment** form.
3. Enter the information into the fields as required.
   - The questions for Threat Analysis and Risk Mitigation are based on the **Risk Assessment Type**.
   - **% of Threat Analysis Assessment Complete** is based on the total number of questions answered.
   - **% of Risk Mitigation Assessment Complete** is based on the total number of questions answered.
   - **Current Unmitigated Risk** and **Current Mitigated Risk** are based on the questions weighted response.

<hint type="info" indent="1">
  Mandatory fields are marked with an asterisk.
</hint>

4. Click **Save**.
5. In the **Threat Analysis** tab, there are a set of questions:
   - For questions that have an impact, select each question, and then select a Threat Analysis Level from the **Select Threat Analysis Level** drop-down list.
   - For questions that have no impact, select each question, and then click **No Impact**.

<hint type="info" indent="1">
  If a Threat Analysis Question is set to **No Impact**, it is removed from the percentage complete. You can select **No Impact** for all but one question, and then select the level of threat and your analysis will be complete.
</hint>

6. In the **Risk Mitigation** tab, there are a set of sample questions.
   - Select a Risk Mitigation Level from the **Select Risk Mitigation Level** drop-down list.
7. Use the **Threat Analysis**, **Risk Mitigation**, **Risks**, **Journals**, **Controls**, **Mitigation Plans**, **Exceptions**, and **Linked Risk Assessments** tabs to link or create new associated records for the Risk Assessment.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Use the **Tasks** tab to create new tasks associated to the Risk Assessment. You cannot link to an existing task from this tab, only create new tasks.
</Paragraph>

<hint type="info" indent="1">
  You can restore hidden tabs using the **plus** sign (to the right of the tabs).
</hint>

8. Click **Save**.

<hint type="info" indent="1">
  Click **Refresh** If changes you made or relevant tabs are not shown in the record after you have saved it.
</hint>

## Edit a Risk Assessment

**To edit a Risk Assessment:**

1. Double-click the Risk Assessment to open the details.
2. Edit the information as required.
3. Click **Save**.
