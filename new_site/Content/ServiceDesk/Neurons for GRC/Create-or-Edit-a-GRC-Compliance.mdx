Create or edit a corrective or preventative Compliance action.

<hint type="info">
  To create or edit a Compliance action, you must log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} as a **GRC Manager**.
</hint>

**To create a Compliance Action:**

1. Open the **Compliance** workspace.
2. Click **New GRC Compliance** to open a blank **Compliance** form.
3. Enter the information into the fields as required.

<hint type="info" indent="1">
  Mandatory fields are marked with an asterisk.
</hint>

4. Click **Save**.
5. In the **Details** tab, enter the information into the field as required.
6. Use the **Activity**, **Standards**, **Assets**, **Tasks**, **Participants**, **Controls**, and **Risk Assessments** tabs to link or add supporting records to the Compliance.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Use the **Activity**, **Tasks** and **Controls** tabs to create new associated records for each type.
</Paragraph>

7. Click **Save**.

<hint type="info" indent="1">
  When the **Status** field of a Compliance action is set to **Accepted**, the owner of the Compliance action is automatically sent a notification email.
</hint>

<hint type="info" indent="1">
  Click **Refresh** If changes you made or relevant tabs are not shown in the record after you have saved it.
</hint>

## Edit a GRC Compliance

**To edit a GRC Compliance:**

1. Double-click a Compliance action to open the details
2. Edit the information as required.
3. Click **Save**.
