\{\{FRS\_VARS.Self\_Service\_Portal-Self-Service}} users can track their \{\{FRS\_VARS.Service\_Requests}} and issues from the My Items workspace. They can access their list of items from their **Home** page in the **My Open Items** pane, or from the **My Items** workspace.

Administrators can view a list of all \{\{FRS\_VARS.My\_Items-My-Items}} for all users, and see the progress of each at a glance.

## Viewing My Items

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Open the **My Items** workspace. A list of records is displayed.
3. Click on a record to view the details.

## Filtering the My Items List

The **My Items** workspace displays a list of all your open items. You can filter this list so you only see the items that you want to see.

<hint type="info">
  \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}} can view the list for all users' items.
</hint>

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Open the \{\{FRS\_VARS.My\_Items-My-Items}} workspace. A list of items appears.
3. In the toolbar area, enter a keyword in the first search text box and press the ENTER key or click the **Start search** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/j6qDv93brOzucQ3n22GGQ_searchicongray.jpg" alt caption size="10" width="15" height="15"}.
