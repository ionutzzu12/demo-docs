The Service Catalog is the backbone of \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}. Setting this up for end users involves many pieces:

- [Working with Services](Creating_a_Service.mdx): Create your overall service, such a transportation or communications.
- [About Request Offerings](../RequestOfferings/About-Request-Offerings.mdx): Create a request offering that is available to users.
- [Working with Service Level Management](About_Service_Level_Management.mdx): Create the service level agreements and service level packages that define your request offering.
- [Working with Service Requests](../ServiceRequest/Service_Requests_.mdx): Once your request offering is published, users see it as a service request.
