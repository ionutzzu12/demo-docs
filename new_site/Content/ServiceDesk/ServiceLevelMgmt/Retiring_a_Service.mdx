title: Retiring a Service

createdAt: Tue Jun 30 2026 17:16:47 GMT+0300 (Eastern European Summer Time)

You can retire a service that is no longer used. Before retiring a service, ensure that it is not being used for incidents, service requests, request offerings, escalations, and so on. Retiring a service could have unexpected consequences.

1. Log in to the application as a service owner.
2. Open the **Services** workspace.
3. Select the service to retire.
4. From the Action Menu, select **No Category > Retire Service**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The system retires but does not delete the service and then lists **Retired&#x20;**&#x6E;ext to its name.
</Paragraph>
