## Viewing Email

1. Log in to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} console.
2. Open the **Email** workspace. A list of emails appears.
3. Double-click an item from the list to view details.

## Viewing a Parent Record

1. Log in to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} console.
2. Open the **Email** workspace. A list of emails appears.
3. Select an email.
4. Click **Open to Parent** from the toolbar.
5. Modify the record as needed.
6. Click **Save**.

## Replying or Replying All to an Email

You can reply or reply all to an email in the **Email** workspace, and add text or attachments.

1. Log in to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} console.
2. Open the **Email** workspace. A list of emails appears.
3. Open the email.
4. Edit the email as needed.
   - Enter any added text into the **Message** text box.
   - **Paste from clipboard**: Pastes any text on the computer clipboard into the message.
   - **Add Attachment**: Adds an attachment to the email.
5. Do any of the following:
   - Click **Reply**. The reply message is sent to the first address listed.
   - Click **Reply All**. The reply message is sent to all addresses listed.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The **Reply** buttons disappear and the message is ready to send.
</Paragraph>

6. Click **Save**. The message is not sent until you save.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  After saving, the buttons appear again.
</Paragraph>

## Forwarding an Email

Emails containing attachments are automatically forwarded with the attachments. You can, however, add additional attachments (limited by server capability).

1. Log in to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} console.
2. Open the **Email** workspace. A list of emails appears.
3. Open the email to forward.
4. Edit the email as needed.
   - Enter any added text into the **Message** text box.
   - **Paste from clipboard**: For on-premise users only. Pastes any text on the computer clipboard into the message. You must have the clipboard data plug-in installed to use this feature. Refer to .
   - **Add Attachment**: Adds an attachment to the email.
5. Click **Forward**. The buttons disappear.
6. Enter the forwarding email address in the **To** box.
7. Click **Save**. The message is not sent until you save.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  After saving, the buttons appear again.
</Paragraph>

## Modifying an Email

1. Log in to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} console.
2. Open the **Email** workspace. A list of emails appears.
3. Open the email to modify.
4. Modify the email.
5. Click **Save**.

## Resending an Email

1. Log in to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} console.
2. Open the **Email** workspace. A list of emails appears.
3. Select the email to resend.
4. From the toolbar, click **Resend email**.

## Using the Internal Email Feature

This feature enables you to test email functionality without requiring external email provider connections.

For information on using this feature, refer to [Setting up Internal Email](../Configure/Email/Email.mdx).
