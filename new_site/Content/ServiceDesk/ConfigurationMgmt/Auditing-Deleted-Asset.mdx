You can conduct audit of your deleted assets from the **Deleted Assets Audit** workspace.

1. log in to \{\{Primary.Product}}.
2. Click **More**, search and open th&#x65;**&#x20;Deleted Assets Audit** workspace.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The history of all assets deleted from the CI workspace is stored here.
</Paragraph>
