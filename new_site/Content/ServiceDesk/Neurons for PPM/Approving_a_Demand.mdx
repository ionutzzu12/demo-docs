Stakeholders identified in the Evaluate phase must approve a Demand so it can move forward. If the budget for a Demand is more than $1 million, it must also be approved by an executive approval group (refer to [Configuring Ivanti Neurons for PPM ](Setting_up_Neurons_for_PPM.mdx)).

Stakeholders are alerted via email of a pending approval.

When all required stakeholders approve the Demand, the status automatically moves to Assessing. During the Assessing Phase, a Project or \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Portfolio\_Manager}} reviews the Demand for inclusion in a Portfolio.
