\{\{FRS\_VARS.Company-short}} \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_for\_PPM}} allows you to search for Resources. You can refine the search by Project Role, Skills, Capacity, and availability. Within this application, availability refers to the employee's set Available Hours and Operational Percent. Capacity refers to the employee's time available to perform the desired Task, taking into account all other Tasks assigned for the specified date range.

Searching works best when you begin with clearly defined Roles, Skills, and a Resource Plan. For more information, refer to:

## Searching for Project Resources

When searching for Project Resources, all searches begin in the Project Phase business object.

1. Click on the **Resourcing** tab.
2. Enter the parameters for your search:

<Paragraph indent="1">
  a. **Start Date**: The earliest date for which your project needs a resource.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  b. **End Date**: The latest date for which your project needs a resource.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  c. **Role**: The Project Role your project needs. The Project Role you choose depends on the Skills your Project needs.
</Paragraph>

3. Click **Link Resources** to search for Resources and link them to the Project Phase.
4. Click **Update Resource Dates**.
5. Click **Update and View Resource Capacity** to create the search results.
6. Click the **Resources** tab to view search results. The **Remaining Capacity** column shows the employee's Capacity to perform the desired Skill.
7. To assign Tasks to the Resources returned in the search, refer to :Link[Creating a Task From a Search]{label="Creating a Task From a Search" overridedLabel="Creating a Task From a Search" spaceId docId="GFDw3UWZa6cQmfDyGu1Kj" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Creating-from-search" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" githubPath="Using_Resource_Capacity.htm#Creating-from-search" href="Using_Resource_Capacity.mdx"}.
