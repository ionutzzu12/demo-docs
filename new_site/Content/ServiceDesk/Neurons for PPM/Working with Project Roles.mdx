\{\{FRS\_VARS.Company-short}} \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_for\_PPM}} allows you to create **Project Roles** and assign them to Resources. Refer to [Working with Roles](../Roles/roles.mdx). Project and \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Portfolio\_Manager}}s assign various **Project Roles** to Resources in order to apply **Project Skills** and manage Resource Capacity. Only \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Project\_Manager}}s and \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Portfolio\_Manager}}s create and apply **Project Roles**. ## Linking Project Roles

After creating a **Project Role**, link it to **Resources** and **Project Skills**. Project and \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Portfolio\_Manager}}s can then search across **Project Roles** for all Resources with the necessary **Project Skills**.

### Linking Project Roles to a Resource

After creating a **Project Role**, link it to individual Resources.

1. Double-click a role from the **Project Roles** workspace list view.
2. Click **Link** on the **Employees** tab to search for an employee.
3. Select **Custom Search**, and enter your search parameters.
4. Click **Search**.
5. Click the name of the employee, and then click **Select** to link the **Project Role** to the employee.

<hint type="info">
  When you select a **Role** in **Projects Roles** workspace, an Employee with same Role set as **Primary Project Role** will be unable to unlink the Role. The **Unlink** option appears disabled.
</hint>

### Linking a Project Role to Project Skills

Often a **Project Skill** applies to everyone assigned a certain **Project Role**. For example, you might apply the skill "SQL Server" to all Resources in the Database Administrator role. When you assign a **Project Role** to a Resource, the **Project Skills** linked to that **Project Role** automatically apply to the Resource.

<hint type="info">
  When you remove a **Project Role** from a Resource, the linked **Project Skills** remain linked to the Resource.
</hint>

1. Double-click on a **Project Role** from the **Project Roles** workspace list view.
2. Click **Link** on the **Project Skills** tab.
3. Click the **Project Skill** to link to the **Project Role**, then click **Select**.
