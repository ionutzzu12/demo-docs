## About Demand Management

Demand Management is the process an organization puts in place to internally collect new ideas, projects, and needs during the creation of a Portfolio.

Demands are called ideas during the initial stages of Demand management. Demands can be created by any \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} user, including \{\{FRS\_VARS.Self\_Service\_Portal-Self-Service}} users. The \{\{FRS\_VARS.Demand\_Manager}} reviews the ideas and can put them into workflow for further detailed evaluation, approval, and consideration for inclusion in a Portfolio. During the detailed evaluation activities, the Demand is scored to help normalize a summary of the information for the approval and Portfolio review activities.

The Demand Management workflow steps through the following statuses:

**Idea**: Demands begin as ideas.

**Evaluating**: A \{\{FRS\_VARS.Demand\_Manager}} adds more detailed information to assess the viability of a Demand. In this phase, you can add supporting information such as a low-level resource plan, benefits, budgets, and risks.

**Awaiting Approval**: Stakeholders identified in the Evaluate phase approve a Demand before it can move forward. If the budget for a Demand is more than $1 million, it must also be approved by an executive approval group.

**Assessing**: After a Demand is approved, it stays in the Assessing status until a \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Project\_Manager}} or \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Portfolio\_Manager}} accepts the Demand into a Portfolio, where it becomes a Project.

## Accessing the Demand Workspace

**To view the&#x20;****Demand****&#x20;workspace:**

1. Log into the application as either a Demand Manager, Project Manager, or Portfolio Manager.
2. Open the **Demand** workspace.

### Search for a Demand

Search for a Demand record by entering the unique Demand ID in th&#x65;**&#x20;Search** field.

1. Enter the Demand ID in the **Search** field at the top right of the Demand workspace.
2. Press **Enter**, or click the **Search** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/n3GQDSo7-8FXTcCZQonV0_searchicon.png" alt caption size="10" width="15" height="17"}.
