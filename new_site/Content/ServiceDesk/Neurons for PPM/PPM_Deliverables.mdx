\{\{FRS\_VARS.Company-short}} \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_for\_PPM}} adds Deliverables as a new business object. Add Deliverables to Programs and Projects to define successful outcomes. A Deliverable business object can only be related to one Project, Portfolio, or Program. Deliverables are not copied when a Project Template is created.

A Deliverable can be either a Process or a Product. A **Process** Deliverable captures the path taken to achieve a result. It can include planning, documentation, information sharing, and Resource planning. A **Product** Deliverable is a final output that directly satisfies one or more of the Project requirements. For example, a **Process** Deliverable might be a **Resource Plan**, while a **Product** Deliverable might be a new release of an application.

**Creating a Deliverable**

1. In the **Deliverables** tab of a Program or Project, click **New Deliverable** to open the **New Deliverables** dialog.
2. Complete the fields for your Deliverable:

<Paragraph indent="1">
  a. **Title**: Enter the name of your Deliverable.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  b. **Type**: Choose **Process** or **Product** from the dropdown menu.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  c. **Due Date**: Click the calendar icon to choose a due date for the Deliverable.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  d. **Status**: Choose **Open** or **Closed** from the dropdown menu.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  e. **Details**: Enter any details you want to record about the Deliverable.
</Paragraph>

3. Click **Save**.
