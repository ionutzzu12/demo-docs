You can create a \{\{FRS\_VARS.Service\_Requests}} on behalf of an external customer and create a relationship between the customer and the request. When you create a \{\{FRS\_VARS.Service\_Requests}}, you can designate the external customer in the **Created By** field.

## Submit a Service Request on behalf of an external customer

The customer must exist as an external contact and appear in the contact list. The external customer might communicate the issue to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}} by email, phone, fax, walk-in, or other means.

1. Log in to Service Desk Console.
2. Open the **Service Request** workspace. A list of \{\{FRS\_VARS.Service\_Requests}} appears.
3. From the toolbar, click **New**. A blank \{\{FRS\_VARS.Service\_Requests}} form appears.
4. In the **Customer** field, enter the name of the customer. A list of matching customer names appears.
5. Select a customer from the list. In this example, we typed *John*, then chose *Katherine Joseph* from the list.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The system automatically generates the values for the **Email** and **Phone** fields.
</Paragraph>

6. In the **Reported by** field, you can enter your name or the name of another customer.
7. Enter other information as needed. Required information is indicated by a red asterisk (<font color="#ff0000">*</font>).

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Note that the **Owner** field automatically shows your logged in ID. You can change this entry if your role has permissions granted by the administrator.
</Paragraph>

8. Click **Save**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The \{\{FRS\_VARS.Service\_Requests}} is now linked to the customer. When viewing the customer records, you can see the list of related \{\{FRS\_VARS.Service\_Requests}}.
</Paragraph>
