## Associating Assets to a Facility

\{\{FRS\_VARS.Company}} \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_for\_Facilities}} provides the ability to associate assets to a facility.

**To associate an asset to a facility:**

1. Log in as either a Facilities Administrator, Facilities Analyst or Facilities Manager role.
2. Open the **Facilities Assets** workspace.
   This displays the **Active Facilities Assets** workspace. Any existing facilities assets are displayed in this workspace.
3. To either create and associate a new asset to a facility, or associate an existing asset to a facility, do one of the following:

<Paragraph indent="1">
  To create and associate a new asset to a facility:
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  a. Click **New** to open a new asset record form.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  b. Enter the required data in the asset summary fields.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  c. Select the **Detail** tab, and as a minimum enter the **Location** and the **Status** for the asset.
  The **Status** field is mandatory. The **Building**, **Floor**, **Room Type**, **Conference Room**, **Cubicle** and **Other Facilities Space** fields are optional, however, you must complete these fields if the asset is to be associated to any of these facilities. Further information on completing these fields is given in the :Link[Facilities Fields]{label="Facilities Fields" overridedLabel="Facilities Fields" spaceId docId="W3UiFv9K8YtILWglY8YIi" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Facilities_Fields" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="Managing_Facilities_Assets.mdx"} table below.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  d. Enter a value (months) in the **Asset Age**/**Useful Life** field, and date in the **In Service Date** field if you want to apply a life cycle for the asset.
  This displays life cycle information for the asset on the dashboard. See :Link[Facilities Assets Dashboard]{label="Facilities Assets Dashboard" overridedLabel="Facilities Assets Dashboard" spaceId docId="W3UiFv9K8YtILWglY8YIi" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Facilities_Assets_Dashboards" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="Managing_Facilities_Assets.mdx"} below.
</Paragraph>

<hint type="info">
  An asset's life cycle begins from the date entered in the **In Service Date** field, not from the date entered in the **Purchase Date** field
</hint>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  e. Click **Save**.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  To associate an existing asset to a facility:
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  a. Select the asset in the **Active Facilities Management** workspace, and then double-click to open the asset's record.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  b. Select the **Detail** tab, and as a minimum enter the **Location** for the asset.
  The **Building**, **Floor**, **Room Type**, **Conference Room**, **Cubicle** and **Other Facilities Space** fields are optional, however, you must complete these fields if the asset is to be associated to any of these facilities. Further information on completing these fields is given in the :Link[Facilities Fields]{label="Facilities Fields" overridedLabel="Facilities Fields" spaceId docId="W3UiFv9K8YtILWglY8YIi" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Facilities_Fields" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="Managing_Facilities_Assets.mdx"} table below.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  c. Enter a value (months) in the **Asset Age**/**Useful Life** field, and date in the **In Service Date** field if you want to apply a life cycle for the asset.
  This displays life cycle information for the asset on the dashboard. See :Link[Facilities Assets Dashboard]{label="Facilities Assets Dashboard" overridedLabel="Facilities Assets Dashboard" spaceId docId="W3UiFv9K8YtILWglY8YIi" version="v2" docAnchorId="#Facilities_Assets_Dashboards" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="Managing_Facilities_Assets.mdx"} below.
</Paragraph>

<hint type="info">
  An asset's life cycle begins from the date entered in the **In Service Date** field, not from the date entered in the **Purchase Date** field
</hint>

<Paragraph indent="1">
  d. Click **Save**.
</Paragraph>

## **Facilities Assets Dashboard**

You can track the an asset's life cycle from the enhanced **Facilities Assets** dashboard. To view this dashboard:

1. Log in as either a Facilities Administrator, Facilities Analyst or Facilities Manager role.
2. Select the **Facilities Assets** dashboard from the **My Dashboard** drop-down list at the top left of the workspace.

The following Facilities Assets dashboard widgets are provided:

- **Facility Assets In Stock**: Displays all assets that are currently held in stock.
- **Facility Assets By Location**: Displays facility assets by their location.
- **Facility Assets Awaiting Disposal**: Lists details of all facilities assets with their record **Status** field marked as **Awaiting Disposal**.
- **Active Work Orders By Statuses**: Displays the status of currently active work orders.
- **Active Shipments By Statuses**: Displays the status of currently active asset shipments.
- **All Active Purchase Orders By Statues**: Displays the status of all currently active purchase orders.
- **Facility Assets Expiring In The Next 30 Days**: Lists all facilities assets that are in use, but their useful life period is due to expire within the next 30 days.
- **Facility Assets End Of Life In Use**: Lists all facilities assets that have met or passed their useful life period, but their record **Status** field is marked as **End of Life In Use**.
- **Facility Assets Reached End Of Life**: Lists all facilities assets that have met or passed their useful life period in relation to their **In Service Date** field.
