After an \{\{FRS\_VARS.Incident}} or Change is closed, the application invites you to participate in a survey. It is important to provide feedback to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} about their effectiveness.

When you receive a survey email, do the following:

1. Click the survey link or copy the survey URL and paste it into the URL field of a browser.
2. Enter your answers to the questions.
3. Click **Submit**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The survey response is stored with the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Incident}}/Change.
</Paragraph>
