The facilities-specific workspaces in \{\{Primary.Company}} \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} are available for \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_for\_Facilities}} roles and functions. The workspaces that are available and accessible to you are based on your user role, and configured for you by your organization's Administrator or Facilities Administrator.

<hint type="info">
  Some of the links provided below navigate to topics under the Out Of The Box (OOTB) version of Facilities Management. The procedures in these topics are the same as used in the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_for\_Facilities}} workspaces.
</hint>

For more information about each workspace, refer to the following topics:

- [Facilities Work Orders](<../Neurons for Facilities/Work-Orders.mdx>): A work order is either a one-time job or scheduled maintenance job that occurs at regular intervals to be done at a facility or site. It involves multiple tasks and checklists to be completed before closing the Work Order. Preventative maintenance schedules can be created within a Work Order.
- **Emergency Plans**: Create an emergency (preparedness) plan that documents the procedures, personnel, and equipment that is relevant to a specific emergency. Refer to [Emergency Plans](<../Neurons for Facilities/Neurons-for-Facilities-Emergency-Plans.mdx>).
- [Facilities Announcement](Facilities-Announcement.mdx): Announcements related to facilities are created and published.
- **Facilities Assets**: An expandable list view of all facility assets. You can create a new asset, delete an asset, associate an asset to a facility, and assign a user, location, and Financial Owner to an asset. Refer to [Managing Facilities Assets](<../Neurons for Facilities/Managing_Facilities_Assets.mdx>).
- [Facilities Requests](Facilities-Requests.mdx): Create and manage requests for facilities work. A facilities request like any other \{\{FRS\_VARS.Service\_Request-Service-Request}} raised by a \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Self\_Service\_User}} user, but related to facilities and owned by the Facilities Management team. All necessary actions are undertaken by the Facilities Management team to fulfill the request.
- [Facilities Location, Building, Floor, Room, and Cubicles](Facilities-Location.mdx): Create and manage locations, buildings, floors, rooms, and cubicles where Work Orders are executed. You can associate these facilities to a Work Order within the Work Orders workspace.
- **Facilities Space**: Create and manage spaces on a floor or within a room. Refer to [Add Facilities Spaces](<../Neurons for Facilities/Neurons-For_Facilities-Spaces.mdx>)
- **Facility Shutdown**: Apply a facility shutdown to make a facility location unavailable due to maintenance, emergencies, holidays, closed hours, or events. Refer to [Scheduled Shutdown](<../Neurons for Facilities/Neurons-for-Facilities-Add-a-Scheduled-Shutdown.mdx>).
- [Facilities Calendars](<../Neurons for Facilities/Neurons-for-Facilities-Calendar.mdx>): Neurons for facilities calendars display information on facility preventative maintenance schedules, shutdowns, and reservation information for rooms, spaces and cubicles.
- [Facilities Knowledge Base](Facilities-KnowledgeBase.mdx): Knowledge articles and questionnaires specific to facilities are created and published in the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Self\_Service\_Portal-Self-Service}} portal.
- [Preventive Maintenance](Preventive-Maintenance.mdx): Scheduled maintenance plans are created and maintained.

<hint type="info">
  Scheduled maintenance can be configured as part of a Work Order. it is not necessary to create a maintenance schedule within the **Preventive Maintenance** workspace.
</hint>

- [Product Catalog](Product-Catalog.mdx): Multiple facilities assets are added to a catalog that can be used on facilities requests.
- [Work Order Projects](Work-Order-Projects.mdx): Large projects are tracked and managed through the different phases, risks, issues, and changes till its completion.
