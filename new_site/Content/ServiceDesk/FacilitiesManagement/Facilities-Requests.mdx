This is a request for facilities which can be raised from the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Self\_Service\_User}} portal or from the Service Desk console.

## Create a new Facilities Request

1. Open the **Facilities Requests** workspace.
2. Enter information into the fields:
   - **Customer**, **Reported By**, and **Description**.
   - **Status**: It is set to **Active&#x20;**&#x69;nitially, and as the Work Order progresses through the workflow, the statuses are updated automatically.
   - **Team**, **Owner&#x20;**&#x61;nd **Urgency**.
3. Click **Save**.

### Facilities Requests Tabs

If you do not see some of the tabs listed here, they may be hidden, click the :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/QguiND0-Jf_eYUcrBPGk3_unhide-tabs.png" alt caption size="10" width="14" height="14"} icon at the right corner of the tabs list and select the tab you wish to reveal.

### Details

You can add notes, send emails, and attach files here.

### To send an email:

1. Click **Add&#x20;**&#x75;nder **Journals&#x20;**&#x61;nd select **Email**.
2. Enter information into fields and click **Ok**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The email is sent to the recipient and the copy is saved in the Journal area.
</Paragraph>

### To add a note:

1. Click **Add&#x20;**&#x75;nder **Journals&#x20;**&#x61;nd select **Notes**.
2. Enter information into fields and click **Ok**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The note is saved in the Journal area.
</Paragraph>

### To attach a file:

1. Click **Attach&#x20;**&#x75;nder **Attachment&#x20;**&#x74;o attach a file or an image.
2. Browse to the file and click **Ok**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The selected file is attached to the Work Order and displayed in the Attachment area.
</Paragraph>

### Activity History

The history of all emails and notes is displayed here. This includes emails you've sent to users, as well as system-generated emails. You can also link to relevant emails associated with other work orders.

### To link or unlink an email:

1. Click **Link&#x20;**&#x61;nd select the email from the list.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The selected email is linked to the Work Order.
</Paragraph>

2. Select the email record and click **Unlink&#x20;**&#x74;o remove the link from the Work Order.

### To send an email

1. Click **New > Email**.
2. Enter information into the fields:
   - **Category**: Select Outgoing.
   - **From**: Auto-filled with the logged-in user's email ID.
   - **From Name:&#x20;**&#x41;uto-filled the logged-in user's name.
   - **To:** The recipient's email ID.
   - **Subject:** The email subject.
   - **Processed:** This checkbox is selected by default, leave it selected.
   - **Publish to Web:&#x20;**&#x53;elect this to publish the email in the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Self\_Service\_User}} portal.
   - **Message:** The email message.
3. Click **Save**.

### To add a note:

1. Click **New > New Notes.**
2. Enter information into the fields:
   - **Category, Source**, and **Summary**.
   - **Duration in mins:** The amount of time spent on the activity.
   - **Publish to Web**: Select this to publish the note in the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Self\_Service\_User}} portal.
3. Click **Save**.

### Approval

All the approvals based on the workflow the Facilities Request goes through is displayed here.

### Task

Add tasks and assign them to relevant teams and owners that can help complete this order. When a task is added, the assigned owner will be notified of the task. The task owner can accept, reject, reassign, or complete the task.

### To add a task:

1. Click **New&#x20;**&#x61;nd enter information into the fields.
   - **Summary&#x20;**&#x61;nd **Details**.
   - **Priority**: Select a value from 1 to 5 based on the priority to want to give to complete the task.
   - **Service**: Select the area which the task belongs.
   - **Team and Owner:&#x20;**&#x41;ssign the team and owner. The owner gets notified of the task and will take necessary action.
   - **Effort and Cost:** The effort and cost is calculated based on the Cost per Minute defined for the team and or owner and the time spent to complete the task.
2. Click **Save**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The task is created and a record is displayed in the **Task&#x20;**&#x74;ab.
</Paragraph>

### External Task

These tasks are the ones that need action from outside the organization. Add tasks to external teams and assign them to relevant owners that can help complete this order. When an external task is added, the assigned owner is notified of the task. The task owner can accept, reject, reassign, or complete the task.

### To add an external task:

1. Click **New&#x20;**&#x61;nd enter information into the fields.
   - **Summary&#x20;**&#x61;nd **Details**.
   - **Priority**: Select a value from 1 to 5 based on the priority to want to give to complete the task.
   - **Service**: Select the area which the task belongs.
   - **Team&#x20;**&#x61;nd **Owner**: Assign the team and owner. The owner gets notified of the task and will take necessary action.
   - **Effort&#x20;**&#x61;nd **Cost**: The effort and cost is calculated based on the Cost per Minute defined for the team and or owner and the time spent to complete the task.
2. Click **Save**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The task is created and a record is displayed in the **Task&#x20;**&#x74;ab.
</Paragraph>

### Fulfillment Item

Items needed to fulfill the Facilities Request can be added and tracked here.

### To add a fulfillment item:

1. Click **New Fulfillment Item**.
2. Enter the information into the fields:
   - **Status**: Set as **Active** initially. Change it based on the action taken to **Completed**, **Failed**, or **Cancelled**.
   - **Name, Description, One Time Price, Recurring Price.**
3. Click **Save**.

### Cost Item

The cost for the item added to Facilities Request can be defined here. You can either add a cost from the existing price list or define new cost.

### To add cost from the price list:

1. Click the **New from Price list** Item.
2. Select a cost from the list.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The selected cost record is displayed.
</Paragraph>

### To define a new cost:

1. Click the **New ITFM Cost Item**.
2. Enter information into the fields:
   - **Item Description**
   - **Price List Item:** Click the import icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/7p6zySGcnRf67fKqDUqBS_importicon.png" alt caption size="10" width="14" height="13"} to import the pricing from an existing item. The costing of this item is applied and all the fields are filled with the information of this item except the Item Description and Unit of Measure.
   - **Unit Cost:** Cost of one unit.
   - **Qty:** Quantity of the item.
   - **Unit of Measure:**
   - **Total Cost:&#x20;**&#x54;he Unit Cost multiplied by the Quantity.
   - **Cost Driver:**
   - **Vendor:** The supplier of the item.
   - **Billable:&#x20;**&#x53;elect checkbox if this is the billable cost.
   - **Capital Expense:** Select checkbox if this is the capital expense.
   - **Fixed Cost**: Select checkbox if this is the fixed cost and no further discount is applicable.
   - **Direct Cost:&#x20;**&#x53;elect checkbox if this is the direct cost.
   - **Notes:&#x20;**&#x45;nter additional notes.
3. Click **Save**.

### Attachment

Attach files and images and references to other websites here. **Click New Attachment** to attach file and click **New URL** to add a website reference.

### Escalation Watch

An escalation occurs when a request's response time exceeds a defined period of time. The application sends notifications to the task owner and team. It's up to the owner and team manager to take appropriate action based on such notifications.

### Audit History

You can view all the changes made to the Facilities Request here, such as the status changes, email records, escalations, links, and unlinks.
