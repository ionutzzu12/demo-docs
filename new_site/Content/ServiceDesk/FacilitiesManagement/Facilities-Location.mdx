Facilities Management involves the location, building, floor, room, and cubicles of the facility. All these are interrelated and it is recommended that you set these up before creating Facilities Assets, Work Orders, and Facilities Requests.

**To add a Facilities Location:**

1. Open the **Facilities Location** workspace.
2. Click **New**.
3. Enter information into the fields.
4. Select the **Facilities Location** checkbox. The location can only be used in Facilities Management if this checkbox is selected.
5. You tag existing Building, Floor, Room, and Cubicles from the respective tabs or create new ones by clicking **New** from the relevant tabs.
6. Click **Save**.

**To add a Facilities Building:**

1. Open the **Facilities Buildings** workspace.
2. Click **New**.
3. Enter information into the fields.
4. You can tag a Floor to the Building or create a new one by clicking **New** under the **Floor** area.
5. Click **Save**.

**To add a Facilities Floor:**

1. Open the **Facilities Floor** workspace.
2. Click **New**.
3. Enter information into the fields.
4. You can tag Cubicles to the Floor or create a new one by clicking **New** under the **Cubicles** area.
5. Click **Save**.

**To add a Facilities Room:**

1. Open the **Facilities Room** workspace.
2. Click **New**.
3. Enter information into the fields.
4. Click **Save**.

**To add Facilities Cubicles:**

1. Open the **Facilities Cubicles** workspace.
2. Click **New**.
3. Enter information into the fields.
4. You can attach an image by clicking **Browse&#x20;**&#x75;nder the **Image** area.
5. Click **Save**.
