A list of Facilities Assets can be added to a Product Catalog which can be used in Facilities Requests.

## To create a Product Catalog

1. Open the **Product Catalog** workspace.
2. Click **New** > **New Facilities Item**.
3. Enter information into the fields:
   - **Item Details** tab: Enter the items details in the fields.
   - **Facilities Assets** tab: Link the assets to the product catalog by clicking **Link**.
4. Click **Save**.

### Product Catalog Tabs

- **Item Details**: The product catalog details such as description, price, expiry date are detailed.
- **Facilities Assets**: The assets are linked and unlinked to the product catalog.
- **Stock Levels**: The list of assets in stock.
- **Active Orders**: Purchase Orders are that are in **Active** status.
- **Approved Vendors**: The list of approved vendors.
- **Journal**: Notes and email are displayed.
- **Attachments**: Files and web references are displayed.
