The Stock Management workspace gives an insight into the list of storage spaces and their locations, and the facility assets stored in location.

## To view the stock

1. Open the **Stock Management** workspace.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  A list of stock records by location are displayed.
</Paragraph>

2. Double-click a record to view the details.
   - **Storage Space Summary**: Displays the Product Catalog details and the asset stock.
   - **Facilities Assets**: Displays the list of Facilities Assets in the stock.
   - **Inventory Transactions**: Displays the inventory's purchase details.
   - **Fulfillment Locations**: Fulfillment location for assets stored in this storage space. Any fulfillment locations listed here are also found in the **Locations** workspace.
   - **Audit History**: Displays the history of asset transactions.
