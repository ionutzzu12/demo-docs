## About Business Value Modeling

You can evaluate different business values. The **Business Value Modeling** workspace shows values based on real data. You can also create your own modeling report.

## Generating the Business Value Model Report

1. Log into the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}}.
2. Open the **Business Value Modeling** workspace.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  From the **Report** drop-down menu are two default reports:
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
  IT Support Performance Report
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
  Service Level Effectiveness
</Paragraph>

3. Choose a report and view the parameters in the table.
4. From the **Date Range** area, enter the start and end date of the data to analyze.
5. Click **Preview**. The application displays the raw data and a corresponding graph at the bottom of the page.
6. To save the data for use as a baseline, click **Save Results**. The application saves the results.

## Creating a New Business Value Modeling Configuration

1. Log into the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}}.
2. Open the **Business Value Modeling** workspace.
3. Click **New Configuration**.
4. Under the **Parameter Details** tab, click **Add Parameter** to begin adding your report parameters.
5. Click **Save**.
