## Auto-Closure of HR Cases

When you change the status of an HR Case to **Cancelled&#x20;**&#x6F;r **Resolved&#x20;**&#x61;nd save it, a timer starts.

After three business days, the HR Case is automatically moved to status **Closed&#x20;**&#x61;ssuming it isn't moved out of status **Resolved** in the meantime.
