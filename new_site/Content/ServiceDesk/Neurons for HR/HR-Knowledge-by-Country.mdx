For information on how to create an HR Knowledge article, refer to [HR Knowledge](../HR/HR-Knowledge.mdx)

**To add or remove a country in an HR Knowledge form:**

1. Open the Document you want to work with.
2. Select **Add Country**.
3. Select your chosen country from the drop-down list.
4. Select **OK**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  Your chosen country appears in the **Country&#x20;**&#x66;ield and can be used for filtering documents.
</Paragraph>

5. To remove a country, repeat the above steps selecting the country you wish to remove from the drop-down list.

**To filter HR Knowledge Articles by country:**

1. Ensure that your role is **Self-Service**.
2. Select **Human Resources** in the Self-Service Portal.

<hint type="info">
  **Note:** Filtering by country is only available for Human Resources knowledge articles and not for IT Services, Facilities or Security.
</hint>

3. Select **Knowledge Base**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The records visible to you are those that match your own country (as a user), and the matching country in the **Country&#x20;**&#x66;ield. For example, if your own country is set to United States in your user details then you see all records with United States in the **Country** field.
</Paragraph>

<hint type="info">
  **Tip:&#xA0;**&#x49;f no country is set in the **Country** field of a Knowledge article, then you see all of those records as well.
</hint>
