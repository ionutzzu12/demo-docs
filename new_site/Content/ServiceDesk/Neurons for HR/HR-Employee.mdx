Use the Employee workspace to add employees to \{\{Primary.Company}} \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_for\_HR}}.

When you create a new employee or view an existing employee record, you see a tab for **HR Details**. This enables you to enter and maintain all the details for an employee in one workspace.
