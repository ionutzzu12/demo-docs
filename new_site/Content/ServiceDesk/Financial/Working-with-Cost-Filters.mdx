1. Log into the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} console.
2. Open the **CI Service** workspace.
3. Open a service record.
4. Click the **Cost Item** tab.
5. To filter the records, click the filter button :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/EE6KQjfpe89oZ1X6wpcMM_filter-button.png" alt caption size="10" width="21" height="22"} next to the desired column name, and select one or more column names.
6. To filter the records specifically by time, select a time period for the start and end dates fields. When you select a time period, the start and end dates are automatically displayed in the fields.
7. After your selections, click **f****ilter**. The records are displayed according to your selection.
8. To remove the filters, click **clear filter**.
9. To group columns, click the filter button :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/EE6KQjfpe89oZ1X6wpcMM_filter-button.png" alt caption size="10" width="21" height="22"} next to the desired column name, and click **Group By This Field**. The items get listed according to the grouped column.
10. To remove the grouping, click the filter button :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/EE6KQjfpe89oZ1X6wpcMM_filter-button.png" alt caption size="10" width="21" height="22"} next to grouped column name, and remove the selection on **Show in Groups**.
11. You can view the total sum for the service on the right-hand side panel. By default, **Separate total for primary cost drivers** is selected.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  *Primary Cost Totals are Separated and Sub-Totaled*
</Paragraph>

12. To move other cost items to the primary cost area, click the **link** icon :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/3aNmOvuWqAurGzGYP6YMW_link.png" alt caption size="10" width="18" height="11"} next to the other cost.
