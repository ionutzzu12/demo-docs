You can :Link[pin]{label="pin" overridedLabel="pin" spaceId docId="NCTV2-nii5m7O79ZmFPVE" version="v2" docAnchorId="#pin-watchlist" loadingMethod="dynamic" newTab="false" href="Watch List.mdx"} and store the frequently-used business objects in a watch list for quick access, similar to bookmarks or favorites. The watch list appears as a button on your top tab bar, and you can add objects to it.

<hint type="info">
  - The watch list is enabled by default for all roles except for the Self Service role.
  - The administrator must set up this feature before it can be used. For more information, see [Configuring Watch List](<Configuring Watch List.mdx>).
</hint>

## Pin or Place the Business Objects in the Watch List

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} portal as an Administrator.
2. From any workspace such as Incident, Service Request, or any other workspace, select a business object record you wish to pin.
3. From the toolbar, click :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/c85Ly3YWDS7DEyTjSTaGn_watchlist-pin.png" alt caption size="10" width="14" height="14"} **Pin It**.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/m4zT9a_EbdEhCPgmQs9Lc_watchlist-pin-selection.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/WatchList-pin-selection.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/WatchList-pin-selection.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

The business object now appears in your watch list.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/-JufaZn87ndoPF7GIDlov_watchlist-filled-items.png" position="flex-start" size="40" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/WatchList-filled-items.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/WatchList-filled-items.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

## Viewing and Editing the Watch List

<hint type="info">
  Enable the *LoadNewWatchListFeature* to access the following features. Refer to [Working with Global Constants](../../Configure/Constants/Defining_Global_Constant.mdx) to define the global constant.
</hint>

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} portal as an Administrator.
2. Click **Watchlist** at the top-right corner of the screen.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The Watch List displays all your most recently accessed items and records and the ones you have pinned to the Watch List.
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/-JufaZn87ndoPF7GIDlov_watchlist-filled-items.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/WatchList-filled-items.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/WatchList-filled-items.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

3. Optionally, you can select **Show more** to view additional items on the Watch List.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/zVzNtbwcYGPaiBLCWVoM3_watchlist-show-more.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/WatchList-show-more.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/WatchList-show-more.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

4. Click the **Edit** button and select the items that you wish to remove from the Watch List. You can also select the checkbox to the bottom of the Watch List to select all the viewed items in the Watch List.
5. Select **Remove Selected** and confirm the prompt to remove.
6. You can also select **Delete All** button to remove all the pinned items from the Watch List.

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/opzbel8UaCidssUtQUPZf_watchlist-show-more-select.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../../Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/WatchList-show-more-select.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/ServiceDesk/WatchList-show-more-select.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

7. Click **Done** to exit the edit mode.
