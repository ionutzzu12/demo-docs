You can export a request, form, or external offering template to your local drive.

1. Log in to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Console}} console and open the **Request Offering** workspace.
2. Click the **Save template on a disk** :inlineImage[]{src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/G8KDPxCeLwJlTjccVKZs0_save-to-disk.png" alt caption size="10" width="20" height="15"} icon next to the request, form, or external offering to save the file as .rox on your local drive, to your "Downloads" folder.

## Importing a Request Offering

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} as a \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Owner}}, and open the **Request Offerings** workspace.
2. Click **Import Offering** from the toolbar. The file page opens.
3. Navigate to the .rox file, then click **Open**.
4. After the request, form, or external offering is imported, click **Save**.
