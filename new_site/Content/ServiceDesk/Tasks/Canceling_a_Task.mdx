---
title: Canceling a Task
docTags: 
createdAt: Tue Jun 30 2026 17:16:48 GMT+0300 (Eastern European Summer Time)
---

You can cancel a task that is not completed.

1. Open a workspace that has tasks. For example, open the **Change** workspace.
2. Open a record within the workspace. For example, open a change.
3. Click the **Task** tab. The system displays the tasks associated with the record.
4. Highlight a task.
5. Click **Cancel** on the task toolbar.
6. Enter the reason for canceling the task.
7. Click **OK**. The system still displays the task in the **Task** workspace, but displays the status as cancelled.
