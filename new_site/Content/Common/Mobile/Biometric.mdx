If you are a \{\{FRS\_VARS.Mobile\_Analyst}} or \{\{FRS\_VARS.Self\_Service\_Mobile\_portal}} user, you can enable biometric authentication to log in to \{\{Primary.Company}} \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} mobile app. This helps you log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} faster and with improved security with a single touch or face scan.

\{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} allows both fingerprint and face ID biometric type, you can choose the biometric type to configure on your mobile device. However, face ID is not supported by \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} on Android devices.

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Enabling biometric authentication on your Neurons for ITSM mobile app

  1. Open the \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} mobile app.
  2. Enter your username and password.
  3. Select the **Enable Biometric Authentication** checkbox and tap **Login**. The biometric prompt is displayed.
  4. Scan your finger or face.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    Once authenticated, you will be logged in to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} mobile app.
  </Paragraph>



  <hint type="info">
    - If your registered fingerprint and face ID is not recognized by the device for some reason, you will be prompted to enter the device's pin code to authenticate.
    - Once you log out from the \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} mobile app, the biometric option will be cleared, you will have to enable it again when you log in.
  </hint>


</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Reopening the Neurons for ITSM mobile app

  If biometric authentication is enabled, when you reopen the \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} mobile app without logging out, the biometric prompt is displayed by default. You can log in by scanning your finger or face and don't have to enter your username or password.
</ExpandableHeading>

<ExpandableHeading>
  ## Logging in to the Neurons for ITSM without biometric authentication

  The biometric prompt is displayed when you reopen the \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} mobile app, however, you can choose to clear the biometric authentication.

  This useful when a user whose fingerprint or face ID is not registered on the mobile device.

  1. Open the \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} mobile app.
  2. Tap outside the biometric prompt pop-up or tap the **Cancel** button.
  3. Clear the existing username. The **Enable Biometric Authentication** checkbox is displayed.
  4. Clear the **Enable Biometric Authentication** checkbox.
  5. Enter your registered \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} username and password.
  6. Tap **login**.

  <Paragraph indent="1">
    You will be logged in to the \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}} mobile app.
  </Paragraph>
</ExpandableHeading>
