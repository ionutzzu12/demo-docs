The application creates a user password whenever a user record is created. Internal user passwords are stored in the user database, but external user passwords are not.

## Changing Your Password

1. Log in to \{\{FRS\_VARS.ProductName}}.
2. Click on your user name or image in the header bar and then select **Change Password**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The **Change Password** dialog box is displayed.
</Paragraph>

3. Enter your old password.
4. Set your new password and confirm it.
5. Click **Change**.
6. Log out and log in again to use the new password.

## Recovering Your Password

<hint type="info">
  - By default, the password recover feature is disabled; the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}} has to enable it from the Configuration console. If you forget your password and are unable to recover it, reach out to your \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}.
  - Password recovery is applicable for internal authorization and does not work for external logins. Ask your administrator about recovering a password when using external authentication.
</hint>

If you forget your password, do the following:

1. Click **Forgot password?** from the main login window.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  *Forgot Password*
</Paragraph>

<Image src="https://archbee-image-uploads-qa.s3.amazonaws.com/yzXkkCTORWlcwRG_AVHKZ/EdbHksBA_-bz3_XiugsL__forgot-password.png" position="flex-start" size="40" indent="1" initialPath="../Resources/Images/Common/Forgot-Password.png" githubPath="Content/Resources/Images/Common/Forgot-Password.png" showCaption="false"></Image>

2. Enter your user name or email address and click **Submit**.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  You will receive a one-time reset link to your registered email address. The link will expire in 20 minutes or as configured by your administrator.
</Paragraph>

3. Click the link. You will be re-directed to the **Reset Password** page.
4. Enter your **User ID**, **New Password** and **Confirm Password**. If you do not know your **User ID**, contact your organization's administrator.
5. Select **Submit**.

<hint type="info">
  If you get this message - "Login Failed. The username or password is incorrect. Please re-type the details and try again." when trying to change or recover your password , then your password might have not reached the minimum age to change. In such a case, reach out to your organization's Administrator to help you recover or change the password.
</hint>
