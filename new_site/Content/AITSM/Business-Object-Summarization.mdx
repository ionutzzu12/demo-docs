The Business Object Summarization capability delivers AI-powered efficiency through advanced summarize AI technology, enabling summarize functionality for faster decision-making. With a single click, the system generates an AI summary by analyzing key fields such as Summary, Description, Urgency, Category, and Journal, creating a clear and concise summary.

This feature helps Service Desk Analysts save time by eliminating the need to manually review multiple fields. Instead, they can quickly understand the context and focus on resolving the issue faster. By reducing effort and improving accuracy, this capability streamlines workflows and enhances overall service desk productivity.

## Configuring the AI Summary button

The **AI Summary** button is not available by default for existing customers; the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}} must specifically add it to the Business Object layout. However, for new users, the **AI Summary** button is available out-of-the-box by default.

**Adding the&#x20;****AI Summary****&#x20;button to the Business Object Layout**

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} as an Administrator.
2. To open a business object, go to Configuration Console > **Build** > **Business Objects**, and then open your preferred business object.
3. Click the **Layouts** tab.
4. Select the Business Object layout used for the role you want the **AI Summary** button implemented.
5. Click **formView** to edit the form.
6. Click **Edit** under the **Toolbar** field. The **Toolbar Editor** page is displayed.
7. Drag the **AI Summary** into the toolbar.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The **Toolbar Button Editor** window is displayed.
</Paragraph>

8. Optionally, under **General** tab, you can customize the AI Summary button according to your preference.
9. Click **Advanced** and enter the following expression:

<CodeblockTabs indent="1">
  ```bash
  $(!IsRegexMatch(GetGlobal("IsAIITSMEnabled"),/"IncidentSummarization":\s*[tT]rue/))
  ```
</CodeblockTabs>

## Using **Business Object Summarization**

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} as an \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}}.
2. Open an Active business object.
3. Click the **AI Summary** button.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The AI-generated summary is displayed on the side panel. The **Summary** section explains the issue and the **History** section details all the interactions to date.
</Paragraph>

<hint type="info">
  - The application takes up to ten seconds to generate the summary.
  - Ensure you provide sufficient details in the **Description** and **Summary** fields to be able to generate the summary.
</hint>
