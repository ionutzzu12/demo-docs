Ivanti Neurons for \{\{FRS\_VARS.ITSM}} introduces \{\{FRS\_VARS.AITSM}} capabilities that simplify the process of creating knowledge content. With just a click, Knowledge Base Articles can be generated using supported out-of-the-box templates, making it easier for Service Desk Analysts to produce consistent and helpful documentation.

This AI knowledge base feature eliminates manual effort, allowing teams to quickly generate knowledge articles and maintain a robust knowledge base platform. Whether you're building a help desk knowledge base or managing KB articles, this intelligent solution enhances productivity and ensures high-quality content across your knowledge base AI environment.

## Configuring the Generate Knowledge Article button

The **Generate Knowledge Article** button is not available by default for existing customers; the \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Administrator-Administrator.}} must specifically add it to the Incident layout. However, for new users, the **Generate Knowledge Article** button is available out-of-the-box by default.

**Prerequisite**

- Existing customers - ensure you import the latest package.

**Adding the Generate Knowledge Article button to the Incident Layout**

1. Log in to \{\{Primary.Product}} as an Administrator.
2. Open the Configuration console > **Build** > **Business Objects** > **Incident** > **Layouts** tab.
3. Select the Incident Business Object layout used for the role you want the **Generate Knowledge Article** button implemented.
4. Click **formView** to edit the form.
5. Click **Edit** under the **Toolbar** field. The **Toolbar Editor** page is displayed.
6. Drag and drop **Generate Knowledge Article** into the toolbar.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The **Toolbar Button Editor** window is displayed.
</Paragraph>

7. Click **Advanced**.
   - Enter the following code under **Hidden Expression**:

```bash
$(!IsRegexMatch(GetGlobal("IsAIITSMEnabled"),/"KnowledgeBaseCreation":\s*[tT]rue/))
```

<Paragraph listStyleType="disc" indent="2">
  Enter the following code under **Disabled Expression**:
</Paragraph>

```bash
$(Status not in ("Resolved", "Closed"))
```

## Generating a Knowledge Base Article

1. Log into \{\{Primary.Product}} as a \{\{FRS\_VARS.Neurons\_ITSM\_-\_Service\_Desk\_Analyst-Service-Desk-Analyst}} or any role having access to the Incident workspace.
2. Open a Resolved or Closed Incident.
3. Click **Generate Knowledge Article** and select a type from the dropdown list.

<Paragraph indent="1">
  The **Knowlege Base Article** is displayed on the side panel. The AI generates the **Title**, **Keywords**, and **Description**.
</Paragraph>

<hint type="info">
  - Only Knowledge Base templates provided out-of-the-box are supported in generating the AI Knowledge Article. Custom templates are not supported.
  - Ensure you provide sufficient details in the **Description**, **Summary**, and **Resolution** fields to be able to generate the knowledge article.
</hint>

4. Select the **Collection** and **Category** and click **Save as Draft**.
5. Click the thumbs up or thumbs down button at the end of the page and enter your comments. Sharing your feedback helps us improve the capability.
